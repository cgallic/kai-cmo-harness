# E-Commerce Marketing Playbook

> **Use when:** Marketing DTC brands, online stores, or any business selling physical/digital products through a website. Covers acquisition, conversion, retention, and growth.

### Google Shopping Setup

- [ ] Google Merchant Center account connected to your store
- [ ] Product feed optimized: titles include keywords, descriptions detailed, images high-quality
- [ ] Shipping and return policies configured
- [ ] Performance Max campaign targeting Shopping
- [ ] Negative keywords: "free", "DIY", "how to" (unless relevant)
- [ ] Product ratings enabled (aggregate review stars in ads)

### Meta Ads for E-Commerce

**Campaign structure:**
```
COLD (60% of budget):
  Broad targeting → let algorithm find buyers
  Creative: product in use, UGC, lifestyle shots
  Objective: Sales (purchase event)

RETARGETING (30% of budget):
  Website visitors, add-to-cart, view-content
  Creative: product photos, reviews, urgency
  Objective: Sales (purchase event)

RETENTION (10% of budget):
  Past customers, email list
  Creative: new arrivals, bundles, loyalty perks
  Objective: Sales (repeat purchase)
```

## Conversion Optimization

### Product Page Checklist

- [ ] High-quality images (5+, multiple angles, lifestyle + product shots)
- [ ] Video showing product in use (increases conversion 80%+)
- [ ] Clear price (no hidden fees until checkout)
- [ ] Prominent "Add to Cart" button (contrasting color, above fold)
- [ ] Size guide / specs table (reduces returns)
- [ ] Customer reviews visible (star rating + written reviews)
- [ ] Shipping info: cost, speed, free shipping threshold
- [ ] Return policy clearly stated (reduces purchase anxiety)
- [ ] Social proof: "1,247 people bought this" or "Only 3 left"
- [ ] Cross-sell: "Frequently bought together" / "You might also like"
- [ ] Trust badges: payment icons, SSL, guarantee badge

### Cart & Checkout Optimization

- [ ] Guest checkout available (don't force account creation)
- [ ] Cart summary visible throughout checkout
- [ ] Shipping costs shown early (surprise fees = #1 abandonment reason)
- [ ] Multiple payment options (credit card, PayPal, Apple Pay, Shop Pay)
- [ ] Buy-now-pay-later option (Klarna, Afterpay) — increases AOV 20-30%
- [ ] Progress indicator (Step 1 of 3)
- [ ] Order summary on final step
- [ ] Mobile-optimized (60%+ of e-commerce traffic is mobile)
- [ ] Address auto-complete
- [ ] Cart recovery: exit-intent popup + abandoned cart emails

### Abandoned Cart Recovery

```
Email 1 (1 hour):  "You left something behind" + product image + link back to cart
Email 2 (24 hours): "Still thinking about it?" + social proof (reviews)
Email 3 (72 hours): Incentive: free shipping, 10% off, or gift with purchase
SMS (4 hours):      "Your cart is waiting — complete your order: [link]"
```

**Benchmark:** Recover 5-15% of abandoned carts with this sequence. At 70% average cart abandonment, that's significant revenue.

## Email Marketing for E-Commerce

### Essential Flows (Automated)

| Flow | Trigger | Emails | Revenue Impact |
|------|---------|--------|---------------|
| **Welcome series** | New subscriber | 3-5 over 7 days | 5-10% of email revenue |
| **Abandoned cart** | Cart abandoned | 3 over 72 hours | 10-20% of email revenue |
| **Post-purchase** | Order placed | 3-5 over 14 days | Cross-sell, reviews |
| **Browse abandonment** | Viewed product, didn't add to cart | 1-2 over 24 hours | 3-5% of email revenue |
| **Win-back** | No purchase in 60-90 days | 3 over 14 days | Re-engage lapsed buyers |
| **VIP / loyalty** | High spenders or frequent buyers | Ongoing | Retention + expansion |
| **Replenishment** | Product has a natural reorder cycle | 1 at predicted reorder time | Repeat purchase |

### Campaign Calendar (Monthly)

| Week | Campaign Type | Example |
|------|-------------|---------|
| Week 1 | New arrivals | "Just dropped: [product line]" |
| Week 2 | Educational / content | "5 ways to style [product]" |
| Week 3 | Social proof | "See what customers are saying" |
| Week 4 | Promotion / urgency | "Last chance: [offer] ends Sunday" |

**Rule:** Max 3-4 emails per week. Mix promotional (40%) with content (40%) and transactional (20%).

## Key E-Commerce Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Good | Excellent |
|--------|---------|------|-----------|
| **Conversion Rate** | Orders / visitors | 2-3% | 4%+ |
| **Average Order Value (AOV)** | Revenue / orders | Growing | — |
| **Customer Acquisition Cost** | Marketing spend / new customers | < 1/3 of first order value | < 1/4 |
| **Customer Lifetime Value** | AOV × purchase frequency × lifespan | > 3× CAC | > 5× CAC |
| **Cart Abandonment Rate** | Abandoned / initiated carts | 65-75% | < 60% |
| **Return Rate** | Returns / orders | 15-25% (apparel) | < 15% |
| **Email Revenue %** | Email revenue / total revenue | 25-35% | 40%+ |
| **Repeat Purchase Rate** | Returning customers / total | 20-30% | 40%+ |
| **Revenue Per Visitor (RPV)** | Revenue / visitors | Growing | — |

### AOV Growth Tactics

| Tactic | How | Impact |
|--------|-----|--------|
| **Free shipping threshold** | "Free shipping on orders $75+" | +10-15% AOV |
| **Bundle deals** | "Buy 2, get 10% off" | +15-25% AOV |
| **Cross-sell on cart page** | "Add [complementary item] for $X" | +5-10% AOV |
| **Upsell on product page** | "Upgrade to premium for $X more" | +10-20% AOV |
| **Buy-now-pay-later** | Klarna, Afterpay | +20-30% AOV |
| **Gift with purchase** | "Free [item] on orders $100+" | +10-15% AOV |
| **Loyalty points** | "Earn 2x points on this order" | +5-10% AOV |

## Seasonal Marketing Calendar

| Season | Key Dates | Strategy |
|--------|----------|----------|
| **Q1** | New Year, Valentine's Day | New Year promos, gift guides |
| **Q2** | Mother's Day, Memorial Day | Gift campaigns, spring sales |
| **Q3** | Back to School, Labor Day | Prep for Q4, clearance |
| **Q4** | Black Friday, Cyber Monday, Christmas | Biggest revenue quarter — plan 2 months ahead |

**Black Friday / Cyber Monday Prep Timeline:**
```
October 1:   Finalize offers, build landing pages
October 15:  Email tease: "Something big is coming"
November 1:  Early access for VIPs / email subscribers
November 15: Increase ad budgets, launch retargeting
BF-CM week:  Full send — email daily, ads at max, social blitz
December 1:  Extend sale or launch "Cyber Week"
December 15: Gift guide push, last-day shipping cutoffs
December 26: New Year / clearance campaign
```
