# Content Repurposing Playbook

> **Use when:** Maximizing distribution from a single piece of content. One pillar → 15-25 assets across platforms.

Sources: [InfluenceFlow Repurposing Guide](https://influenceflow.io/resources/repurposing-content-across-multiple-platforms-the-complete-2026-guide/), [Koanthic Multi-Channel Guide](https://koanthic.com/en/content-repurposing-ai-complete-multi-channel-guide-2026/), [Vibbit Video Repurposing](https://vibbit.ai/blog/ai-video-content-repurposing-strategy)

---

## The Core Principle

**1 pillar → 15-25 channel-specific assets.** Instead of creating 10 separate pieces, create 1 strong pillar and spend 1-2 hours adapting it. Marketers who repurpose see 40% more output and 25-35% more engagement from reinforced messaging across channels.

---

## The Pillar-to-Platform System

### Step 1: Create the Pillar

A pillar is your highest-effort content piece. It contains all the ideas — everything else is extracted from it.

**Best pillar formats:**
| Format | Why It Works | Output Potential |
|--------|-------------|-----------------|
| Long-form blog (2000+ words) | Rich with extractable sections, data, quotes | 15-20 assets |
| Video (15-30 min) | Clips, audio, transcript, quotes, thumbnails | 20-30 assets |
| Webinar / live stream | Interactive, Q&A generates bonus content | 25-40 assets |
| Podcast episode (30-60 min) | Audio, transcript, clips, quotes | 15-25 assets |
| Original research / report | Data points, charts, findings | 20-30 assets |

**Write pillars with repurposing in mind:**
- Self-contained sections (each H2 = one social post)
- Extractable data points (stats that stand alone)
- Quotable frameworks (diagrams, formulas, lists)
- Clear takeaways per section (not just one conclusion)

### Step 2: The Extraction Matrix

From one 2000-word blog post, extract:

```
PILLAR: "7 Ways Law Firms Lose Clients to Voicemail"
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SHORT-FORM VIDEO (4-6 pieces):
  □ Hook reel: "47% of law firm calls go to voicemail. Here's what that costs."
  □ Tip reel per section: "Way #1: After-hours calls" (15-30s each)
  □ Data reveal reel: "The average missed call costs $450"
  □ Transformation reel: Before (missed calls) → After (AI answers)

SOCIAL POSTS (5-8 pieces):
  □ LinkedIn text post: Full section #1 rewritten as standalone insight
  □ X/Twitter thread: All 7 ways as a numbered thread
  □ X/Twitter single: The most surprising stat as a standalone tweet
  □ Instagram carousel: 7 slides, one way per slide, CTA on slide 8
  □ LinkedIn carousel/document: Same as Instagram, reformatted

EMAIL (2-3 pieces):
  □ Newsletter edition: Summary + link to full post
  □ Nurture email: One section as a standalone value email
  □ Cold outreach hook: Lead with the data point, link to research

AUDIO (1-2 pieces):
  □ Podcast talking points: Use the 7 ways as discussion outline
  □ Audiogram: 60-second clip of the most compelling point

VISUAL (2-3 pieces):
  □ Infographic: Visual summary of all 7 ways + data
  □ Quote graphic: The most shareable stat on a branded template
  □ Chart/data viz: The cost-of-missed-calls data as a visual

COMMUNITY (1-2 pieces):
  □ Discussion prompt: "Which of these 7 ways hits hardest for you?"
  □ Poll: "How do you handle after-hours calls?" with options

REPURPOSED CONTENT (2-3 pieces):
  □ Guest post angle: Rewrite for an industry publication
  □ Quora/Reddit answer: Answer a related question, cite your research
  □ SlideShare/Google Slides: Turn into a presentation

TOTAL: 20-25 pieces from 1 pillar
```

### Step 3: Platform Adaptation Rules

**Don't just copy-paste.** Each platform has a native format:

| Platform | Adaptation Rule | Format |
|----------|---------------|--------|
| **LinkedIn** | Professional framing, longer text, personal insight added | Text post (150-300 words) or carousel |
| **X/Twitter** | Compress to punchy statements, thread for depth | Thread (7-10 tweets) or single tweet |
| **Instagram** | Visual-first, text on image/video, casual tone | Reel (15-30s) or carousel (5-10 slides) |
| **TikTok** | Hook in 1 second, fast-paced, trend-aware | Vertical video (15-30s) |
| **YouTube** | Expand into a fuller narrative, add examples | Long-form (8-15 min) or Short (30-60s) |
| **Email** | Personal tone, one key insight, single CTA | 200-400 words |
| **Podcast** | Conversational expansion, stories, tangents welcome | 15-45 min discussion |
| **Reddit/Quora** | Answer a question, cite data, be genuinely helpful | 200-500 word answer |

## Content Repurposing Calendar

### Weekly Workflow (for 1 pillar/week)

| Day | Activity | Output |
|-----|----------|--------|
| **Mon** | Publish pillar content (blog/video/podcast) | 1 pillar live |
| **Mon** | Extract social posts + schedule for the week | 5-8 posts queued |
| **Tue** | Create short-form video clips from pillar | 3-5 Reels/TikToks |
| **Wed** | Write email newsletter featuring the pillar | 1 email sent |
| **Thu** | Create carousel/visual assets | 2-3 visual pieces |
| **Fri** | Cross-post to communities (Reddit, Slack, Discord) | 2-3 community posts |
| **Weekend** | Batch-create next week's pillar | 1 pillar drafted |

### Monthly Cadence

```
Week 1: Pillar A → repurpose across all channels
Week 2: Pillar B → repurpose across all channels
Week 3: Pillar C → repurpose across all channels
Week 4: REMIX WEEK — take best-performing pieces from weeks 1-3,
        create new angles, deeper dives, or combine insights

Monthly total: 3 pillars → 60-75 pieces of content
```

## The Repurposing Decision Tree

```
DID THE PILLAR PERFORM WELL?
  │
  ├── YES (top 20% by engagement/traffic)
  │   ├── Create MORE derivative content from it
  │   ├── Turn it into a different pillar format (blog → video, or video → blog)
  │   ├── Refresh and republish in 3-6 months with updated data
  │   └── Use it as the basis for a paid ad creative
  │
  └── NO (underperformed)
      ├── Was the topic wrong? → Don't repurpose, move on
      ├── Was the format wrong? → Try a different pillar format (blog didn't work → try video)
      └── Was the distribution wrong? → Try different channels/times with same content
```

## Metrics

| Metric | What It Measures | Target |
|--------|-----------------|--------|
| Content output multiplier | Pieces published / pillars created | 10-20x |
| Time per derivative piece | Minutes to adapt a pillar to one platform | < 15 min |
| Cross-platform reach | Total impressions across all channels from 1 pillar | Growing MoM |
| Best-performing format | Which derivative format gets most engagement | Track and double down |
| Pillar → conversion attribution | Do derivatives drive traffic back to pillar → conversion? | Measurable via UTMs |
