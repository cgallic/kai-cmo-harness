# Podcast Marketing Playbook

> **Use when:** Launching, growing, or monetizing a podcast as a marketing channel. Covers strategy, guest acquisition, distribution, growth tactics, and repurposing. For equipment/recording setup, see `channels/podcast.md`.

---

## Why Podcast in 2026

- **Trust-building channel** — long-form audio builds deeper relationships than any other format
- **SEO compound returns** — episode pages + transcripts rank for long-tail keywords
- **Network effect** — every guest promotes the episode to their audience
- **Low competition** — 4M+ podcasts exist but only ~400K are active (most quit after 7 episodes)
- **Revenue diversification** — sponsorships, premium content, lead generation, product sales
- **Repurposing goldmine** — one 45-min episode yields 20-30+ content pieces (see `playbooks/content-repurposing.md`)

## Podcast Strategy

### Format Selection

| Format | Best For | Effort | Growth Potential |
|--------|----------|--------|-----------------|
| **Solo / monologue** | Thought leadership, niche expertise | Low | Medium (depends on your reach) |
| **Interview / guest** | Network building, borrowed audience | Medium | High (guest promotes to their audience) |
| **Co-hosted** | Chemistry, recurring banter, community | Medium | Medium-High |
| **Panel / roundtable** | Industry commentary, multiple viewpoints | High | Medium |
| **Narrative / storytelling** | Brand building, entertainment | Very High | Highest (but hardest to produce) |

**Recommendation for marketing podcasts:** Interview format. Each guest brings their audience. Your network grows episode by episode.

### Episode Length

| Length | Best For | Listener Retention |
|--------|----------|-------------------|
| 15-25 min | Quick tips, news commentary | High completion (70%+) |
| 30-45 min | Standard interviews, topic deep dives | Good completion (50-60%) |
| 60-90 min | Long-form conversations, expert guests | Lower completion but deeper engagement |
| 90+ min | Only if the conversation warrants it (Joe Rogan model) | Niche loyal audience |

**Sweet spot for B2B:** 30-45 minutes. Long enough for depth, short enough for a commute.

### Naming & Positioning

- **Specific > clever** — "The SaaS Marketing Show" beats "Paradigm Shift Conversations"
- **Include your category keyword** — helps with Apple Podcasts / Spotify search
- **Target one niche** — "Marketing for law firms" > "Marketing tips for everyone"
- **Avoid names that require explanation** — if you have to explain the name, it's wrong

## Guest Acquisition

### Who to Target

```
TIER 1 (Build credibility):
  Industry leaders with 10K+ following
  Authors of relevant books
  Founders of companies your audience admires
  → Harder to book, massive reach, 1-2 per quarter

TIER 2 (Build audience):
  Practitioners with 1K-10K following
  Active LinkedIn/X creators in your niche
  Consultants and agency owners
  → Easier to book, good reach, 2-4 per month

TIER 3 (Build community):
  Your customers with great stories
  Rising practitioners, new voices
  Peers at your level
  → Easiest to book, authentic stories, 2-4 per month
```

### Guest Outreach Template

```
Subject: Guest invite — {Podcast Name} (episode on {topic})

Hi {name},

I host {Podcast Name} — a show about {niche} for {audience}.
We have {social proof: download numbers, notable past guests, audience size}.

I'd love to have you on to talk about {specific topic related to their expertise}.
Your {specific thing they did/wrote/built} was {genuine compliment + why it matters}.

Episodes are {length}, recorded via {Riverside/Zoom}, and we handle all editing.
We'll promote to our {audience size} across {channels}.

Would you be open to a {length} conversation in the next few weeks?

{Name}
{Podcast link}
```

### Guest Prep Kit (send after booking)

Include:
- Episode topic + 3-5 questions (so they can prepare, not scripted)
- Recording logistics (platform, date, time, timezone)
- Technical requirements (quiet room, headphones, mic if possible)
- What to expect (intro format, how long, when it airs)
- Promotion ask: "We'll send you assets to share with your audience"

## Production Workflow

### Weekly Schedule (1 episode/week)

| Day | Activity | Time |
|-----|----------|------|
| **Monday** | Record episode | 60-90 min |
| **Tuesday** | Edit audio + video (or outsource) | 2-3 hours (or $50-150/episode outsourced) |
| **Wednesday** | Write show notes, create clips, schedule social | 1-2 hours |
| **Thursday** | Publish episode + send to email list | 30 min |
| **Friday** | Engage with listeners, share guest's promotion | 30 min |

### Editing Checklist

- [ ] Remove long pauses, filler words ("um", "uh"), false starts
- [ ] Normalize audio levels between speakers
- [ ] Add intro music + branding (10-15 seconds max)
- [ ] Add outro with CTA (subscribe, leave a review, visit website)
- [ ] Export at 128kbps MP3 (standard for podcast hosting)
- [ ] Create video version with captions (if video podcast)
- [ ] Generate transcript (Descript, Whisper, or podcast host auto-transcription)

### Tools

| Tool | Purpose | Cost |
|------|---------|------|
| **Riverside.fm** | Remote recording (studio quality, separate tracks) | $15+/mo |
| **Descript** | Edit by editing the transcript + auto-remove filler | $24+/mo |
| **Buzzsprout / Transistor** | Podcast hosting + distribution | $12-19/mo |
| **Headliner** | Create audiograms for social | Free tier |
| **OpusClip** | AI-clip long episodes into shorts | $15+/mo |
| **Castmagic** | AI: episode → blog, social, email, show notes | $23+/mo |
| **Podcastle** | AI audio enhancement + editing | Free tier |

## Distribution

### Where to Publish

| Platform | Audience | How |
|----------|----------|-----|
| **Apple Podcasts** | 30-40% of podcast listeners | Via RSS feed from your host |
| **Spotify** | 30-35% of listeners | Via RSS or Spotify for Podcasters |
| **YouTube** | Fastest-growing podcast platform | Upload video version manually |
| **YouTube Music** | Emerging platform | Via RSS from YouTube |
| **Google Podcasts** | Deprecated → YouTube Music | Redirect |
| **Amazon Music / Audible** | 5-10% | Via RSS |
| **Your website** | SEO value | Embed player + full transcript |

**YouTube is now critical.** Upload every episode as a YouTube video (even if audio-only with a static image). YouTube is the #1 podcast discovery platform for Gen Z and growing across all demographics.

### SEO for Podcasts

Each episode should have a dedicated page on your website with:
- [ ] Episode title with keyword (H1)
- [ ] Full transcript (massive SEO value — 5,000+ words of keyword-rich content)
- [ ] Show notes with timestamps and links
- [ ] Embedded audio player
- [ ] Embedded YouTube video (if applicable)
- [ ] Guest bio with link to their site
- [ ] Related episode links (internal linking)
- [ ] CTA (subscribe, newsletter, product)
- [ ] Schema markup (PodcastEpisode schema)

## Growth Tactics

### Phase 1: Launch (Episodes 1-10)

| Tactic | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Launch with 3-5 episodes (not 1) | Gives new listeners binge content |
| Tell your existing network (email, social, personal) | First 50-100 listeners |
| Ask every guest to share | Guest's audience discovers you |
| Submit to all directories on day 1 | Maximum discoverability |
| Ask for reviews early (Apple Podcasts) | Rankings boost |

### Phase 2: Grow (Episodes 10-50)

| Tactic | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| **Guest-powered growth** — book guests with bigger audiences | Each guest = new exposure |
| **Cross-promotion** — swap promos with similar podcasts | Access each other's listeners |
| **Clip strategy** — post 3-5 short clips per episode on social | Discovery on Instagram/TikTok/X |
| **Newsletter** — email every episode to your list | Consistent listeners |
| **YouTube optimization** — titles, thumbnails, chapters | YouTube search discovery |
| **Transcripts + blog posts** — SEO from every episode | Organic traffic compounds |
| **Appear on other podcasts** — be a guest yourself | Reverse guest acquisition |

### Phase 3: Scale (50+ Episodes)

| Tactic | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| **Live episodes** — record in front of an audience | Community building |
| **Premium content** — bonus episodes, early access | Monetization |
| **Podcast network** — launch complementary shows | Brand expansion |
| **Sponsorships** — sell sponsorship slots | Revenue |
| **Community** — Discord/Slack for listeners | Retention + engagement |
| **Events** — in-person events for listeners | Deepest engagement |

## Monetization

### Revenue Streams (by podcast size)

| Size | Revenue Model | Expected Revenue |
|------|-------------|-----------------|
| **< 1,000 downloads/episode** | Product promotion, lead gen, affiliate | $0-500/month (indirect) |
| **1,000-5,000 downloads** | Sponsorships, premium content | $500-3,000/month |
| **5,000-20,000 downloads** | Sponsorships, courses, events | $3,000-15,000/month |
| **20,000+ downloads** | Multiple sponsors, premium, licensing | $15,000+/month |

### Sponsorship Pricing

```
CPM (Cost Per Mille = per 1,000 downloads):
  Pre-roll (15-30s, start of episode):    $15-25 CPM
  Mid-roll (60s, middle of episode):      $25-50 CPM
  Post-roll (15-30s, end of episode):     $10-15 CPM

Example: 5,000 downloads/episode × $30 CPM mid-roll = $150/episode
  With 4 episodes/month = $600/month from one sponsor
  With 2 sponsors = $1,200/month
```

### Lead Generation (most valuable for B2B)

Even without direct monetization, a podcast generates:
- **Inbound leads** — listeners who become customers (track with "How did you hear about us?")
- **Network access** — guests become partners, advisors, or customers
- **Content engine** — each episode feeds blog, social, email, SEO
- **Authority** — "Host of [Podcast]" is a credibility signal in sales

## Repurposing (from one episode)

```
ONE 45-MINUTE EPISODE
  │
  ├── Full video → YouTube (long-form)
  ├── 3-5 short clips → TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, X
  ├── Full transcript → Blog post (SEO optimized)
  ├── Key quotes → 5-8 social media posts (LinkedIn, X)
  ├── Key insights → Newsletter edition
  ├── Audio highlights → Audiogram for social
  ├── Guest quote cards → Instagram carousel, LinkedIn document
  ├── Episode summary → Email to subscriber list
  └── Behind-the-scenes → Instagram Stories

  TOTAL: 20-30 pieces from 1 recording session
```
