# Video Content Creation — Production Playbook

> **Use when:** Creating video content for any platform — social media, ads, YouTube, landing pages, or product demos.

---

## Video Content Hierarchy

```
HIGHEST IMPACT → LOWEST IMPACT
1. Short-form social (Reels, TikTok, Shorts) — reach + awareness
2. YouTube long-form — authority + SEO + evergreen
3. Testimonial videos — trust + conversion
4. Product demos — consideration + education
5. Webinar recordings — lead gen + authority
6. Podcast video — relationship building
```

---

## Short-Form Video (15-60 seconds)

### The Hook Framework (first 1-3 seconds)

Every short video lives or dies by its hook. Proven hook types:

| Hook Type | Example | When to Use |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| **Contrarian** | "Everyone's wrong about SEO in 2026" | Hot takes, thought leadership |
| **Curiosity gap** | "This one change doubled our conversion rate" | Results, tutorials |
| **Direct address** | "If you're a lawyer losing calls, watch this" | Targeted content |
| **Pattern interrupt** | Unexpected visual/sound in first frame | Scroll-stopping |
| **POV** | "POV: You just discovered AI answering service" | Relatable scenarios |
| **List** | "3 things I wish I knew before launching ads" | Educational content |
| **Before/After** | Show transformation immediately | Results, tutorials |

### Script Template (15-second)

```
[0-1s]  HOOK: "{One sentence that stops the scroll}"
[1-3s]  CONTEXT: "{Why this matters to the viewer}"
[3-10s] VALUE: "{The actual insight/demo/tip}"
        {Cut every 2-3 seconds to maintain pace}
[10-13s] PROOF: "{Quick stat, testimonial, or result}"
[13-15s] CTA: "{Follow / Save / Link in bio / Comment}"
```

### Script Template (30-second)

```
[0-2s]  HOOK: "{Pattern interrupt + bold claim}"
[2-5s]  PROBLEM: "{Name the specific pain point}"
[5-15s] SOLUTION: "{Step-by-step or demo}"
        {3-4 cuts, text overlays at each transition}
[15-25s] PROOF: "{Result + social proof}"
[25-30s] CTA: "{Specific action — not 'check link in bio'}"
```

### Script Template (60-second)

```
[0-3s]   HOOK: "{The boldest version of your claim}"
[3-8s]   SETUP: "{Context — who this is for, why now}"
[8-25s]  CONTENT A: "{First main point with example}"
[25-40s] CONTENT B: "{Second point or deeper dive}"
[40-50s] PROOF: "{Data, testimonial, demonstration}"
[50-55s] RECAP: "{One-sentence summary of the value}"
[55-60s] CTA: "{Clear next step}"
```

## Long-Form Video (5-20 minutes)

### YouTube Video Structure

```
INTRO (30s-2min):
  - Cold open: start mid-story or with the payoff
  - Don't say "Hey guys, welcome to my channel" — start with value
  - Promise: "By the end of this video, you'll know how to..."

CONTENT (bulk):
  - Break into 3-5 chapters with timestamps
  - Each chapter: claim → evidence → example → transition
  - Re-hook every 3-4 minutes ("Now here's where it gets interesting...")
  - Use pattern interrupts (b-roll, graphics, cuts) every 30-60 seconds

CONCLUSION (1-2min):
  - Summary of key points (not a rehash — one line each)
  - CTA: specific next video or playlist
  - End screen: clickable cards + subscribe
```

### Retention Killers (avoid these)
- Long intros (>30 seconds before value)
- "Don't forget to like and subscribe" in the first minute
- Monotone delivery (vary pace, volume, emphasis)
- Static camera with no cuts for >60 seconds
- Promising one thing, delivering another (clickbait backfires)

## Testimonial Videos

### The 60-Second Testimonial Formula

```
[0-5s]   WHO: Name, title, company
[5-15s]  BEFORE: "We were struggling with..."
[15-30s] DISCOVERY: "Then we found {product}..."
[30-45s] RESULT: "Now we {specific outcome with numbers}..."
[45-55s] RECOMMENDATION: "If you're dealing with {pain}, I'd say..."
[55-60s] SIGN-OFF: Natural ending, smile
```

### Interview Questions (for pulling good testimonials)
1. "What were you doing before you found us?"
2. "What was the moment you decided to try {product}?"
3. "What specific result surprised you the most?"
4. "If a friend asked you about {product}, what would you say?"
5. "What would you tell someone who's on the fence?"

Never ask: "Did you like our product?" (leads to generic answers)

## Production Quality Tiers

### Tier 1: Phone-Quality (UGC)
- **Equipment:** Smartphone + natural lighting + lavalier mic ($20)
- **Time:** 15-30 minutes including editing
- **Best for:** TikTok, Instagram Reels, testimonials, BTS content
- **Secret:** This tier often OUTPERFORMS studio quality on social (authenticity > polish)

### Tier 2: Prosumer
- **Equipment:** Mirrorless camera + ring light + wireless mic ($500-1000)
- **Time:** 1-2 hours per video
- **Best for:** YouTube tutorials, product demos, talking head content
- **Apps:** CapCut (free), DaVinci Resolve (free), Descript ($24/mo)

### Tier 3: Professional
- **Equipment:** Full studio setup, professional lighting, multiple cameras
- **Time:** Half-day to full-day production
- **Best for:** Brand campaigns, hero content, TV/streaming ads
- **When:** Only when the content will be used for 6+ months and across multiple channels

## Editing Best Practices

### Pacing
- Social (15-60s): Cut every 2-3 seconds
- YouTube (5-20 min): Cut every 5-8 seconds (more breathing room)
- Testimonials: Minimal cuts (authenticity > pace)

### Text Overlays
- Always add text overlays for social (85% watch sound-off)
- Font: Bold, high contrast (white on dark, or outlined)
- Position: Top-center or center (avoid bottom where platform UI overlays)
- Timing: Match spoken word timing (not too fast, not too slow)

### Music
- Always add background music (even subtle — it reduces perceived silence)
- Match energy to content (upbeat for tutorials, calm for thought leadership)
- Volume: -15 to -20dB behind voice
- Licensing: Use royalty-free libraries (Epidemic Sound, Artlist) or platform-provided

### Thumbnails (YouTube)
- Custom thumbnail for every video (never use auto-generated)
- Face + emotion + 3-4 words of text
- Bright, high-contrast colors
- Must be legible at 120x68 pixels (mobile search size)
- A/B test thumbnails (YouTube now supports this natively)

## Repurposing Framework

One video → 10+ pieces of content:

```
LONG-FORM VIDEO (YouTube, 15 min)
  │
  ├── 3-5 short clips (Reels, TikTok, Shorts)
  │     Each clip = one key point from the video
  │
  ├── Audiogram → Podcast episode
  │
  ├── Transcript → Blog post (with additional SEO optimization)
  │
  ├── Key quotes → Social media posts (LinkedIn, X)
  │     Pull 5-10 quotable lines, each becomes a post
  │
  ├── Screenshots → Carousel post (Instagram, LinkedIn)
  │     Key frames + text overlay = educational carousel
  │
  └── Behind-the-scenes → Stories content
        Setup, bloopers, process
```

### Automated Repurposing Tools
- **OpusClip** — AI auto-clips long videos into shorts
- **Repurpose.io** — Auto-posts clips to multiple platforms
- **Descript** — Transcript-based editing, auto-removes filler words
- **Headliner** — Creates audiograms with waveform visualization
