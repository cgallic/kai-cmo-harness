# Influencer Marketing Playbook

> **Use when:** Planning or executing influencer partnerships, UGC creator campaigns, or ambassador programs.

---

## Influencer Tiers

| Tier | Follower Range | Avg Engagement | Cost | Best For |
|------|---------------|----------------|------|----------|
| Nano | 1K-10K | 5-8% | $50-500/post | Hyper-niche, authentic, high trust |
| Micro | 10K-100K | 3-5% | $500-5K/post | Niche authority, strong engagement |
| Mid-tier | 100K-500K | 2-3% | $5K-25K/post | Broader reach, still relatable |
| Macro | 500K-1M | 1-2% | $25K-100K/post | Mass awareness, brand lift |
| Mega | 1M+ | 0.5-1.5% | $100K+/post | Celebrity, massive reach |

**Rule of thumb:** 10 nano influencers > 1 macro influencer for conversion. Nano influencers have higher trust with their audience and better engagement rates.

## Campaign Types

### Product Seeding
- Send free product to 20-50 creators
- No obligation to post (but most will if the product is good)
- Cost: Product cost only
- Best for: Physical products, new launches, building relationships

### Sponsored Content
- Pay creator to produce and post content featuring your product
- Give creative freedom (their audience knows their style)
- Include FTC disclosure requirements in the brief
- Best for: Awareness campaigns, specific messaging needs

### UGC (User-Generated Content)
- Pay creators to produce content YOU own and use in ads
- Creator doesn't need to post on their account
- Typically $150-500 per video for nano/micro creators
- Best for: Ad creative that feels authentic, testimonial-style ads

### Affiliate/Performance
- Pay commission on sales generated through creator's unique link/code
- Lower risk (pay only for results)
- Typical commission: 10-30% of sale
- Best for: DTC products, subscription services, clear purchase funnel

### Ambassador Programs
- Long-term partnership (3-12 months)
- Monthly fee + commission + perks
- Creator becomes a genuine advocate
- Best for: Building long-term brand association

## Finding the Right Creators

### Research Methods
1. **Hashtag search** — Search your niche hashtags on Instagram/TikTok, find who's creating in your space
2. **Competitor analysis** — Who are your competitors working with? (check their tagged posts)
3. **Platform tools** — TikTok Creator Marketplace, Instagram Brand Collabs Manager
4. **Third-party tools** — Upfluence, Grin, CreatorIQ, Aspire
5. **Your own audience** — Who's already talking about you? (organic advocates are best)

### Vetting Criteria
- [ ] Engagement rate > 2% (below suggests fake followers)
- [ ] Comments are genuine (not just emojis or bots)
- [ ] Content quality matches your brand standards
- [ ] Audience demographics align with your target persona
- [ ] No brand safety issues (controversial content, competing brands)
- [ ] Previous sponsored content looks natural (not jarring)

## Creator Brief Template

```
INFLUENCER BRIEF
════════════════

Campaign:      {campaign name}
Product:       {product being featured}
Deliverables:  {1 TikTok video + 3 Instagram Stories}
Timeline:      Content due by {date}, post by {date}
Compensation:  ${amount} + {product/affiliate code}

KEY MESSAGES (pick 2-3, use your own words):
  1. {Message 1 — e.g., "Answer every call in 0.4 seconds"}
  2. {Message 2 — e.g., "No more missed clients"}
  3. {Message 3 — e.g., "Free 14-day trial, no card required"}

MUST INCLUDE:
  - Product name mentioned naturally
  - CTA: "Link in bio" or "Use code {CODE} for X% off"
  - #ad or #sponsored (FTC requirement)

MUST AVOID:
  - Making medical/legal/financial claims
  - Comparing to specific competitors by name
  - Before/after claims without substantiation

CREATIVE DIRECTION:
  Style: {Natural/authentic, not scripted}
  Tone: {Conversational, as if recommending to a friend}
  Length: {15-60 seconds}
  Format: {Vertical video}

APPROVAL:
  Draft review: Yes / No
  Revision rounds: {1-2 max}
  Final approval deadline: {date}
```

## Legal & Compliance

### FTC Requirements (US)
- **All** sponsored content must be disclosed
- Disclosure must be clear and conspicuous
- "#ad" at the beginning of the caption (not buried in hashtag list)
- Verbal disclosure in videos ("This is sponsored by...")
- Platform's built-in "Paid Partnership" label when available
- Disclosure required even for free products (gifted)

### Contract Must-Haves
- [ ] Content ownership/usage rights (can you use their content in ads?)
- [ ] Exclusivity period (can they work with competitors?)
- [ ] Content approval process and timeline
- [ ] Payment terms and schedule
- [ ] Revision policy (how many rounds, what's included)
- [ ] Cancellation/kill fee clause
- [ ] FTC compliance clause (they're responsible for disclosure)

## Measuring Influencer ROI

### Direct Attribution
- Unique discount codes per creator
- UTM-tagged links per creator
- Dedicated landing pages per creator
- Pixel tracking on conversion pages

### Indirect Attribution
- Brand search volume lift during campaign
- Social media follower growth
- Share of voice in your niche
- Engagement rate on your own content

### Benchmarks (by platform)

| Metric | Instagram | TikTok | YouTube |
|--------|-----------|--------|---------|
| CPM (cost per 1K views) | $5-15 | $3-10 | $10-30 |
| Engagement rate (good) | 3%+ | 5%+ | 3%+ |
| Click-through rate | 0.5-1% | 0.5-1.5% | 1-3% |
| Conversion rate (from click) | 2-5% | 1-3% | 3-8% |
