# Product Launch Playbook — Marketing Execution Guide

> **Use when:** Launching a new product, feature, or major campaign. Covers pre-launch, launch day, and post-launch optimization.

---

## Launch Tiers

| Tier | Budget | Timeline | Examples |
|------|--------|----------|---------|
| **Tier 1: Soft Launch** | $0-500 | 1-2 weeks | Feature update, blog series, email to existing list |
| **Tier 2: Standard Launch** | $500-5K | 2-4 weeks | New product, rebrand, major feature |
| **Tier 3: Full Campaign** | $5K-50K | 4-8 weeks | Market entry, competitive displacement, category creation |

---

## Pre-Launch (2-4 weeks before)

### Week -4: Foundation

- [ ] Define launch goals (specific metrics: signups, revenue, press mentions)
- [ ] Identify target persona (pick ONE primary persona)
- [ ] Craft core message (one sentence: what it is + who it's for + why now)
- [ ] Create landing page with email capture
- [ ] Set up tracking (UTMs, conversion events, attribution)

### Week -3: Content Preparation

- [ ] Write launch blog post / announcement
- [ ] Create email sequence (announcement → feature breakdown → social proof → urgency)
- [ ] Prepare social media posts (day-of + 7 days after)
- [ ] Create ad creatives (run `/creative-brief` for direction)
- [ ] Record demo video or product walkthrough
- [ ] Prepare press release (if Tier 2-3)

### Week -2: Audience Warming

- [ ] Tease on social media ("Something's coming..." with specific date)
- [ ] Send "insider preview" to VIP customers / beta testers
- [ ] Brief internal team (everyone should be able to explain the launch in 1 sentence)
- [ ] Test all landing pages, forms, checkout flows
- [ ] Set up monitoring dashboards (real-time during launch)

### Week -1: Final Prep

- [ ] Proofread ALL copy (run `/content-gate` on everything)
- [ ] Test email deliverability (send to yourself on different providers)
- [ ] Load ad campaigns in platforms (but don't activate)
- [ ] Confirm pricing / offer is correct everywhere
- [ ] Prepare launch day communication schedule (hour by hour)
- [ ] Have a "war room" plan (who monitors what)

## Launch Day Execution

### Hour-by-Hour Schedule

```
6:00 AM  — Activate landing page changes
6:30 AM  — Send launch email (Batch 1: VIPs and early access)
7:00 AM  — Post on LinkedIn (founder's personal account)
7:15 AM  — Post on X/Twitter (thread format)
7:30 AM  — Activate paid ads (all platforms)
8:00 AM  — Post on Instagram (Reel or carousel)
9:00 AM  — Send launch email (Batch 2: full list)
10:00 AM — Monitor first metrics (email open rate, site traffic, ad spend)
12:00 PM — Social media engagement round (reply to all comments)
2:00 PM  — Submit to Product Hunt / Hacker News (if applicable)
4:00 PM  — Send reminder email to non-openers
6:00 PM  — Evening social post (different angle from morning)
9:00 PM  — End-of-day metrics check
```

### Launch Day Monitoring Dashboard

Track in real-time:
- Website traffic (by source)
- Signup/conversion count
- Email open rate + click rate
- Social media engagement
- Ad spend + CPA
- Customer support volume (is anything broken?)

### If Something Goes Wrong

| Issue | Response | Timeline |
|-------|----------|----------|
| Site down | Roll back, notify on social "we're fixing it" | Immediate |
| Checkout broken | Pause ads, fix, resume | Within 30 min |
| Email not sending | Switch to backup ESP or delay batch 2 | Within 1 hour |
| Ad disapproved | Fix copy, resubmit, run backup creative | Within 2 hours |
| Negative feedback | Acknowledge publicly, fix privately | Same day |
| Low performance | Don't panic — wait 48 hours before major changes | 48 hours |

## Post-Launch (Days 1-30)

### Day 1-3: Monitor and React
- Check metrics daily against goals
- Reply to every customer interaction (social, email, support)
- Fix any broken flows immediately
- Collect early feedback and testimonials

### Day 4-7: Optimize
- Kill underperforming ad variants
- A/B test landing page if conversion rate < target
- Send follow-up email sequence to non-converters
- Create "missed it?" social content for late audience

### Day 8-14: Amplify
- Publish case study from early adopters
- Launch retargeting campaigns (site visitors who didn't convert)
- Pitch press / media with early results
- Create user-generated content campaign

### Day 15-30: Learn
- Run `/content-report` to see what performed
- Run `/content-retro` to extract patterns
- Document what worked and what didn't
- Plan Phase 2 based on learnings

## Launch Checklist (Summary)

### Pre-Launch
- [ ] Goals defined with specific metrics
- [ ] Target persona identified
- [ ] Core message crafted (1 sentence)
- [ ] Landing page live with tracking
- [ ] Content suite prepared (blog, email, social, ads, video)
- [ ] All copy passed quality gate (`/content-gate`)
- [ ] Platform compliance checked for all ads (`/ad-copy`)
- [ ] Internal team briefed
- [ ] Monitoring dashboard set up

### Launch Day
- [ ] Landing page activated
- [ ] Email sent (VIP batch → full list)
- [ ] Social posts published (all platforms)
- [ ] Paid ads activated
- [ ] Real-time monitoring active
- [ ] Team on standby for issues

### Post-Launch
- [ ] Day 1-3 metrics reviewed
- [ ] Underperforming ads killed
- [ ] Follow-up email sequence sent
- [ ] Early testimonials collected
- [ ] Retargeting campaigns launched
- [ ] 30-day retro completed (`/content-retro`)
