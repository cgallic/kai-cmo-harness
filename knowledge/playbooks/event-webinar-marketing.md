# Event & Webinar Marketing Playbook

> **Use when:** Running webinars, virtual events, conferences, workshops, or any event-driven lead generation. 95% of B2B marketers say webinars are key to their strategy.

Sources: [ClickMeeting B2B Webinars](https://blog.clickmeeting.com/using-webinars-for-b2b-marketing), [Gudsho Webinar Lead Gen](https://www.gudsho.com/blog/webinar-lead-generation/), [Kacerr Webinar Marketing Guide](https://www.kacerr.com/webinar-marketing-guide-2026/)

| **Meetup (local)** | 10-50 | High | Medium | Community, local presence |

## Webinar Playbook

### 4 Weeks Before: Planning

**Topic selection:**
- Solve a specific problem your ICP has (not a product pitch)
- "How to [achieve outcome]" > "[Product] overview"
- Check: "Would I attend this if a competitor hosted it?" If no, pick a better topic.

**Format decision:**
| Format | When | Duration |
|--------|------|----------|
| Solo presentation | Expert thought leadership | 30-45 min |
| Panel discussion | Multiple perspectives, bigger names | 45-60 min |
| Interview / fireside | Guest has a big name or story | 30-45 min |
| Workshop / demo | Hands-on, practical audience | 60-90 min |
| AMA / Q&A | Community engagement | 30-45 min |

**Co-marketing opportunity:** Partner with a complementary company. Both promote → double the audience. See `playbooks/partnership-comarketing.md`.

**Registration page checklist:**
- [ ] Clear title + specific benefit ("Learn how to X" not "Join our webinar")
- [ ] Date, time, timezone prominently displayed
- [ ] Speaker bio + headshot (credibility)
- [ ] 3-5 bullet points of what attendees will learn
- [ ] Form fields: Name, Email, Job Title, Company (max 4-5 fields)
- [ ] Calendar add button (Google, Outlook, iCal)
- [ ] Social proof if available ("500+ marketers already registered")

### 2 Weeks Before: Promotion

**Email sequence:**
```
Week -2:  Announcement email to full list
Week -1:  Reminder email + share on social media
Day -3:   "3 days away" email to registered + non-registered
Day -1:   "Tomorrow!" email to registered only
Hour -1:  "Starting soon" email with join link
```

**Promotion channels:**
| Channel | Tactic | Expected Registrations |
|---------|--------|----------------------|
| Email list | 2-3 emails to full list | 5-15% of list |
| LinkedIn | Personal post from speaker + company post | 20-50 |
| X/Twitter | Thread about the topic + registration link | 10-30 |
| Partner promotion | Co-host emails their list | 50-200 |
| Paid ads (LinkedIn/Meta) | Target job title + interest | $5-20 per registrant |
| Community (Slack/Discord) | Share in relevant communities | 10-30 |

**Benchmarks:**
- Registration-to-attendee rate: 35-45% (typical), 50%+ (excellent)
- Aim for 2-3x your target attendance in registrations

### Day Of: Execution

**Technical checklist:**
- [ ] Test audio, video, screen share 30 min before
- [ ] Have backup internet (phone hotspot)
- [ ] Close all notifications and unnecessary apps
- [ ] Co-host manages chat, Q&A, and tech issues
- [ ] Recording is ON (verify)
- [ ] Slides loaded and tested

**Engagement tactics during the webinar:**
- **Poll** at minute 5 (breaks the ice, gets them participating)
- **Chat prompt** at minute 15 ("Drop your biggest challenge with [topic] in the chat")
- **Q&A** — collect throughout, address in final 10-15 min
- **Resource drop** — share a link to a valuable resource mid-webinar (captures engagement)
- **CTA** — final 3 minutes, specific next step (not a hard sell)

**Webinar structure (45 min):**
```
[0-5 min]   Intro: who you are, what they'll learn, housekeeping
[5-10 min]  Hook: the problem/opportunity that brought them here
[10-30 min] Content: 3-5 key points with examples, data, frameworks
[30-35 min] CTA: what to do next (trial, demo, resource)
[35-45 min] Q&A: answer top questions from chat
```

### Post-Webinar: Follow-Up

**Immediate (within 2 hours):**
- Send recording + slides to all registrants (attendees AND no-shows)
- Include a specific CTA (not just "here's the recording")

**Day 1-2:**
- Email attendees: "Thanks for joining — here's the key takeaway + next step"
- Email no-shows: "Sorry we missed you — here's the recording"

**Day 3-5:**
- Segment by engagement:
  - Stayed 30+ min + asked questions = hot lead → sales outreach
  - Stayed 15-30 min = warm lead → nurture email with related content
  - Registered but didn't attend = cold → send recording + re-engage

**Day 7-14:**
- Related content email (blog post, case study on same topic)
- Offer follow-up: demo, trial, or consultation

**Repurpose (ongoing):**
- Cut into 3-5 short video clips for social
- Write blog post from webinar content
- Create carousel from key slides
- Add to on-demand library
- See `playbooks/content-repurposing.md`

## Event Marketing (In-Person & Virtual Summits)

### Conference Sponsorship Checklist

- [ ] Audience matches your ICP (verify attendee demographics)
- [ ] Booth location: high-traffic area (near food, keynote stage, entrance)
- [ ] Booth assets: banner, demo stations, swag, lead capture method
- [ ] Pre-event outreach: email target accounts attending
- [ ] Speaking slot: apply for a panel or presentation (10x ROI over booth alone)
- [ ] Post-event follow-up plan: contact all leads within 48 hours
- [ ] ROI tracking: cost vs leads vs pipeline vs revenue

### Virtual Summit Playbook

```
PLANNING (8 weeks out):
  - Define theme and audience
  - Recruit 8-12 speakers (partner companies, industry experts)
  - Build registration page
  - Each speaker promotes to their audience (the distribution model)

PROMOTION (4 weeks out):
  - Speaker promotion kit: email swipe copy, social posts, graphics
  - Your promotion: email, social, paid ads
  - Track registrations by source (which speaker drives the most?)

EXECUTION:
  - 1-2 day event
  - 4-8 sessions, 30-45 min each
  - Live chat and Q&A per session
  - Recording available for 48-72 hours (urgency)

FOLLOW-UP:
  - Send recordings to all registrants
  - Segment by session attended → targeted follow-up
  - Co-marketing with speakers on post-event content
```

## Webinar Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Good | Excellent |
|--------|---------|------|-----------|
| Registration rate | Registrations / landing page visitors | 30-50% | 55%+ |
| Attendance rate | Attendees / registrations | 35-45% | 50%+ |
| Engagement rate | Polls + chat + Q&A participants / attendees | 40-60% | 70%+ |
| Avg watch time | Avg minutes watched / total webinar length | 50-65% | 75%+ |
| Drop-off rate | Attendees who leave before end / total | 30-50% | < 25% |
| Lead-to-MQL rate | MQLs generated / total registrants | 15-25% | 30%+ |
| Pipeline generated | $ pipeline from webinar leads (90 days) | Track | Growing QoQ |
| Cost per registrant | Total promotion cost / registrations | $5-25 | < $10 |
| Cost per attendee | Total cost / attendees | $15-50 | < $25 |
