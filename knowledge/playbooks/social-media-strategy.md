# Social Media Strategy — Cross-Platform Playbook

> **Use when:** Planning or executing organic social media strategy across Instagram, LinkedIn, X/Twitter, YouTube, or TikTok.

---

## Platform Strategy Matrix

| Platform | Best For | Post Frequency | Content Type | Audience |
|----------|----------|----------------|-------------|----------|
| LinkedIn | B2B thought leadership, recruiting, partnerships | 3-5x/week | Long-form text, carousels, documents | Professionals, decision-makers |
| Instagram | Brand building, visual storytelling, DTC | 5-7x/week (stories daily) | Reels, carousels, stories | 18-44, lifestyle/visual |
| X/Twitter | Real-time engagement, hot takes, tech/startup | 3-10x/day | Short text, threads, quote tweets | Tech, media, culture |
| TikTok | Virality, Gen Z/Millennial reach, culture | 1-3x/day | Short video (15-60s) | 16-34, entertainment-first |
| YouTube | Long-form education, SEO, evergreen content | 1-2x/week | Videos (8-20 min), Shorts | Search-driven, all ages |

## Content Pillars Framework

Every brand needs 3-5 content pillars. All content maps to one.

```
PILLAR 1: Expertise (30%)
  - How-to content
  - Industry insights
  - Data/research
  Purpose: Build authority

PILLAR 2: Behind-the-Scenes (20%)
  - Team, process, culture
  - Building in public
  - Failures and lessons
  Purpose: Build trust

PILLAR 3: Community (20%)
  - User stories / testimonials
  - Customer wins
  - Q&A / responses
  Purpose: Build belonging

PILLAR 4: Entertainment (15%)
  - Memes, trends, humor
  - Hot takes
  - Cultural moments
  Purpose: Build reach

PILLAR 5: Product (15%)
  - Features, updates
  - Use cases
  - Comparisons
  Purpose: Build pipeline
```

**Rule: Never let Product exceed 20% of total posts.** Social media is not a billboard.

## Platform-Specific Best Practices

### LinkedIn

**What works in 2026:**
- Personal profiles outperform company pages 5-10x on reach
- Text-only posts with strong hooks get more impressions than link posts
- Carousels/documents get highest saves (algorithm signal)
- Commenting on others' posts builds reach faster than posting

**Post structure:**
```
Line 1: Hook (pattern interrupt — stat, question, hot take)
Line 2: Empty line (forces "see more" click)
Lines 3-10: The story/insight (short paragraphs, 1-2 sentences each)
Last line: CTA or question to drive comments
```

**Engagement hacks:**
- Reply to every comment within 1 hour (boosts distribution)
- Ask genuine questions (not "thoughts?" — be specific)
- Tag people only when genuinely relevant (not engagement bait)
- Post between 7-9 AM or 12-1 PM local time (commute + lunch)

### Instagram

**What works in 2026:**
- Reels are the #1 reach driver (algorithm prioritizes video)
- Carousels have highest save rate (10-slide education format)
- Stories keep existing followers engaged (don't grow audience)
- Collab posts double reach (shared to both audiences)

**Reels formula:**
```
[0-1s]  HOOK — text overlay, pattern interrupt
[1-3s]  CONTEXT — what this is about in 2 seconds
[3-12s] VALUE — the actual content (fast-paced, cuts every 2-3s)
[12-15s] CTA — "Follow for more" or "Save this"
```

**Hashtag strategy:**
- 5-15 hashtags per post (down from 30)
- Mix: 3 broad (1M+ posts), 5 medium (10K-500K), 5 niche (<10K)
- Use hashtags as SEO keywords (Instagram search is growing)

### X/Twitter

**What works in 2026:**
- Threads > single tweets for engagement
- Contrarian takes + data get the most retweets
- Quote tweets with added insight > plain retweets
- Spaces (live audio) builds loyal following fast

**Thread structure:**
```
Tweet 1: Hook + promise ("7 things I learned scaling to $1M ARR:")
Tweet 2-7: One point per tweet, concrete examples
Tweet 8: Summary + CTA ("Follow @handle for more. RT tweet 1 if this was useful.")
```

**Engagement rules:**
- Tweet minimum 3x/day (consistency > quality for growth)
- Reply to 10-20 accounts in your niche daily (genuine replies, not "great point!")
- Post between 8-10 AM and 5-7 PM (commute times)
- Use polls for easy engagement (algorithm boost)

### TikTok (Organic)

**What works in 2026:**
- Educational content ("I was today years old when I learned...") dominates
- Trends matter but niche > trend-chasing for sustainable growth
- First 1-2 seconds determine 90% of performance
- Reply videos (stitching/dueting) get more reach than standalone

**Algorithm signals (ranked by importance):**
1. Watch time % (most important — people need to watch most of the video)
2. Rewatches (indicator of high-value content)
3. Shares (strongest engagement signal)
4. Comments > Likes (comments weight more)
5. Profile visits after watching (intent signal)

**Content cadence:**
- Post 1-3 times per day (algorithm rewards consistency)
- Use trending sounds (even at low volume behind voiceover)
- Keep videos 15-30 seconds for best completion rate
- Hook format: "Here's why [counterintuitive claim]" or "POV: [relatable scenario]"

### YouTube

**What works in 2026:**
- Titles and thumbnails determine 80% of a video's success
- Retention curve matters more than views (YouTube promotes high-retention)
- YouTube Shorts drive subscribers but not watch time revenue
- Community posts boost channel visibility between uploads

**Video structure:**
```
[0-30s]    HOOK: Why should they watch? (teaser, not intro)
[30s-2m]   SETUP: Context they need to understand the value
[2m-end-2] CONTENT: Deliver on the promise, structured in chapters
[Last 2m]  CTA: Subscribe, next video, playlist
```

**Thumbnail rules:**
- Faces with emotion outperform text-only 2:1
- 3 elements max on any thumbnail
- Contrast: must be readable at 120x68 pixels (mobile search)
- Never put important info in bottom-right (timestamp overlay)

**SEO for YouTube:**
- Title: keyword first, then hook ("AI Receptionists: How Law Firms Save 12 Hours/Week")
- Description: first 2 lines matter (shown in search), keyword-rich
- Tags: deprecated but still use 5-10 relevant ones
- Chapters: add timestamps for key sections (improves retention + search)

| Day | Platform | Content Type | Pillar |
|-----|----------|-------------|--------|
| Mon | LinkedIn | Industry insight thread | Expertise |
| Tue | X/Twitter | Hot take + data | Expertise |
| Wed | LinkedIn | Behind-the-scenes | BTS |
| Thu | Instagram | Reel (educational) | Expertise |
| Fri | X/Twitter | Thread (tactical how-to) | Expertise |
| Sat | Instagram | Carousel (tips/list) | Community |
| Sun | — | Rest / batch next week | — |

### Batch Production Workflow
1. Monday: Plan week's content (30 min)
2. Tuesday: Write all copy for the week (1 hour)
3. Wednesday: Create visual assets (1 hour)
4. Thursday: Schedule everything (30 min)
5. Daily: 15 min engagement (reply to comments, engage on others' posts)

## Metrics That Matter

### Vanity vs. Value Metrics

| Vanity (nice to know) | Value (drives decisions) |
|----------------------|------------------------|
| Follower count | Engagement rate (engagements / reach) |
| Likes | Click-through rate to site |
| Impressions | Conversions from social |
| Post reach | Revenue attributed to social |

### Platform Benchmarks (2026)

| Metric | LinkedIn | Instagram | X/Twitter | TikTok |
|--------|----------|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Engagement rate | 2-4% | 1-3% | 0.5-1.5% | 3-8% |
| Avg reach/post | 5-15% of followers | 10-30% of followers | 2-8% of followers | Variable (FYP) |
| Link CTR | 1-3% | 0.5-1% (stories) | 0.5-2% | 0.5-1% |
| Best post type | Text, carousels | Reels, carousels | Threads, hot takes | Educational, trends |
