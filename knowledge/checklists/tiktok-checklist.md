# TikTok Shop Content Checklist

> **Use when:** Creating shoppable videos for TikTok Shop.

## Product Selection
- [ ] Passes 3-Second Mute Test (visual benefit understood)
- [ ] Has "Wow Factor" or visual appeal
- [ ] Solves real problem (not manufactured)
- [ ] Has brand story or differentiation
- [ ] Commission/margin makes sense

## 6-Step Script
- [ ] **Step 1 (0-3s):** Keyword said AND shown in text overlay
- [ ] **Step 2 (3-15s):** Soft CTA mentions shop/price
- [ ] **Step 3 (15-30s):** Problem agitation with validation
- [ ] **Step 4 (30-45s):** Wow demonstration (visual proof)
- [ ] **Step 5 (45-50s):** FOMO/scarcity anchor
- [ ] **Step 6 (50-60s):** Hard CTA pointing to cart

## Filming
- [ ] 3-4 hook variations recorded
- [ ] A-Roll: Creator holding product (hook)
- [ ] Macro B-Roll: Close-up of texture/details
- [ ] Action Shot: Product in use
- [ ] Reaction Shot: Creator satisfaction

## Technical Quality ("Good Quality" Tag)
- [ ] Lighting: Product well-lit, no shadows/grain
- [ ] Audio: Clear and crisp (lavalier mic)
- [ ] Text in safe zones (not over Like/Comment/Share)
- [ ] Resolution: HD minimum

## Editing ("Clean Cut" Method)
- [ ] No dead air (all breaths cut)
- [ ] Visual changes every 2-4 seconds
- [ ] Keyword in text overlay (first 3 seconds)
- [ ] Soft CTA within 15 seconds
- [ ] No talking head during demonstration

## Posting & SEO
- [ ] Caption rich in keywords (not just emoji)
- [ ] 3-5 specific hashtags (#coffeelover not #fyp)
- [ ] No generic hashtags (#fyp, #viral, #foryou)
- [ ] Posted at optimal time

## Audit Questions
- [ ] Did I say AND show keyword in first 3s?
- [ ] Did I mention shop/price within 15s?
- [ ] Is product demonstrated in action?
- [ ] Are hashtags niche-specific?
- [ ] Any medical/weight loss claims? (violation risk)
