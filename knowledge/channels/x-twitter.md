## Content Strategy

### Post Types (Ranked by Reach)

| Type | Reach | Engagement | Effort |
|------|-------|-----------|--------|
| **Threads (3-10 tweets)** | Highest | Highest | Medium |
| **Hot takes / opinions** | High | High | Low |
| **Quote tweets with insight** | High | Medium | Low |
| **Data/charts/screenshots** | Medium-High | Medium | Medium |
| **Polls** | Medium | Very High | Low |
| **Single tweets** | Medium | Low-Medium | Low |
| **Links to external content** | Lowest | Low | Low |

**Note:** X penalizes external links in reach. If sharing a blog post, put the insight in the tweet and add the link in a reply, not the main post.

### Posting Cadence

| Stage | Frequency | Strategy |
|-------|-----------|---------|
| Building (0-1K followers) | 3-5 tweets/day | Engage 10x more than you post. Reply to big accounts in your niche. |
| Growing (1K-10K) | 5-10 tweets/day | Mix original content + replies + threads. |
| Established (10K+) | 5-15 tweets/day | Content machine — threads, takes, data. Delegate replies. |

## Thread Strategy

Threads are the highest-reach format on X. A well-written thread can get 10-100x the engagement of a single tweet.

### Thread Structure

```
TWEET 1 (HOOK):
  Bold claim, surprising stat, or promise
  "I analyzed 500 landing pages. Here are the 7 patterns that convert at 10%+:"
  (This tweet gets retweeted — make it self-contained and compelling)

TWEETS 2-8 (CONTENT):
  One point per tweet
  Each tweet should be valuable standalone (people read out of order)
  Use numbers: "1/" "2/" etc. for structure
  Include screenshots, data, or examples where possible

TWEET 9 (SUMMARY + CTA):
  "TL;DR:" one-line summary
  "If this was helpful, RT the first tweet so more people see it"
  "Follow @handle for more [topic]"
```

### Thread Hooks That Work

| Hook Pattern | Example |
|-------------|---------|
| **List promise** | "10 things I wish I knew before launching my startup:" |
| **Contrarian** | "Everything you've been told about SEO is wrong. Here's why:" |
| **Story** | "In 2024, I was broke. In 2026, we're doing $2M ARR. Here's what happened:" |
| **Analysis** | "I spent 40 hours studying the top 50 SaaS landing pages. Here's what I found:" |
| **Framework** | "The framework that took us from 0 to 10K customers:" |
| **Failure** | "Our biggest marketing mistake cost us $50K. Here's what we learned:" |

## Growth Tactics

### The Engagement-First Strategy

1. **Find 20 accounts** in your niche with 10K-100K followers
2. **Turn on notifications** for their posts
3. **Reply within 15 minutes** of their posts with thoughtful comments (not "great post!")
4. **Add value** — share data, a contrarian angle, or a relevant example
5. **Do this daily** — 10-20 quality replies/day
6. Their audience sees your replies → follows you → your tweets reach more people

This works because X's algorithm surfaces highly-engaged replies.

### Content Recycling

Your followers see ~5% of your tweets. Recycling is not just acceptable — it's necessary.

- **Recycle top tweets** every 30-60 days (reword slightly)
- **Thread → single tweets**: Break threads into standalone tweets posted over weeks
- **Screenshots**: Turn other platforms' data/insights into screenshot tweets
- **Quote your own old tweets**: Add new context or update

### Spaces (Live Audio)

- Host 1-2 Spaces per month on a niche topic
- Co-host with someone who has a bigger audience (their followers get notified)
- Record and repurpose as podcast content
- Push notification goes to ALL followers → massive reach spike

## X Ads

### Ad Formats

| Format | Best For | Specs |
|--------|----------|-------|
| Promoted Tweets | Engagement, awareness | 280 chars + media |
| Video Ads | Brand awareness, reach | 15-30s recommended, autoplay |
| Carousel Ads | Multiple products/features | 2-6 cards |
| Follower Ads | Audience building | Profile card format |

### Targeting Options
- **Keywords** — target users who tweeted about specific topics
- **Follower look-alikes** — target followers of specific accounts
- **Interests** — broad category targeting
- **Tailored audiences** — upload email lists, website visitors
- **Conversation targeting** — topics people are actively discussing

### X Ad Tips
- **Verification matters** — verified accounts get more ad impressions
- **Promoted tweets that look organic** perform best (not obviously an ad)
- **Thread-style promoted tweets** get higher engagement than single-tweet ads
- **Avoid external links in the main tweet** — link in reply or use a card format
