# Affiliate & Referral Marketing Channel Guide

> **Use when:** Building an affiliate program, referral program, or partnership-based distribution channel.

---

## Affiliate vs Referral

| | Affiliate | Referral |
|---|----------|---------|
| **Who** | Third-party marketers, publishers, influencers | Existing customers |
| **Motivation** | Commission (money) | Reward (credits, discounts, cash) |
| **Scale** | Potentially massive (thousands of affiliates) | Limited to customer base |
| **Trust** | Variable (depends on affiliate quality) | High (friend recommendation) |
| **Setup** | Affiliate platform + tracking + payouts | Referral code/link + reward system |
| **Cost** | 10-30% of sale (CPA model) | Product cost of reward |

## Affiliate Program Playbook

### Program Structure

| Element | Recommendation |
|---------|---------------|
| Commission rate | 15-30% of first sale (SaaS), 10-20% (physical products) |
| Cookie duration | 30-90 days (longer = more attractive to affiliates) |
| Payment schedule | Monthly, NET 30 |
| Minimum payout | $50-100 |
| Recurring commission | Yes for SaaS (as long as customer stays = affiliate earns) |
| Attribution | Last-click with 30-90 day cookie |

### Tier Structure (for scaling)

```
BRONZE (0-5 referrals/month):
  Commission: 15%
  Support: Self-serve dashboard

SILVER (5-20 referrals/month):
  Commission: 20%
  Support: Monthly check-in call

GOLD (20+ referrals/month):
  Commission: 25-30%
  Support: Dedicated affiliate manager
  Perks: Early access to features, co-marketing opportunities
```

### Affiliate Recruitment

**Where to find affiliates:**
1. **Your customers** — happy customers are the best affiliates (they already believe in the product)
2. **Industry bloggers** — people who write about your niche
3. **YouTubers** — "best [category] tools" videos drive affiliate revenue
4. **Comparison sites** — G2, Capterra, TrustRadius (some accept affiliate links)
5. **Affiliate networks** — ShareASale, Impact, PartnerStack, CJ Affiliate
6. **Podcast hosts** — niche podcasts with sponsored segments
7. **Newsletter operators** — niche newsletters with affiliate sections
8. **Course creators** — people who teach in your space

**Outreach template:**
```
Subject: Partner with [Product] — [Commission]% recurring commission

Hi {name},

I noticed you [specific thing they did — review, article, video] about [topic].

We're launching an affiliate program for [product] and I think your audience
would genuinely benefit from it. Here's what we offer:

- {Commission}% recurring commission (you earn as long as they're a customer)
- 60-day cookie (generous attribution window)
- Dedicated affiliate dashboard with real-time tracking
- Custom landing pages and creative assets
- Priority support for your referred customers

[Product] does [1-sentence value prop]. We have [social proof — customer count, rating].

Want me to set up your affiliate account? Takes 2 minutes.

{Name}
```

### Affiliate Assets to Provide

- [ ] Unique tracking link per affiliate
- [ ] Custom discount code (if applicable)
- [ ] Banner ads in standard sizes (300x250, 728x90, 160x600)
- [ ] Email swipe copy (3-5 emails they can adapt)
- [ ] Social media posts (copy + images ready to use)
- [ ] Product screenshots and logos
- [ ] Comparison chart (you vs competitors)
- [ ] FAQ / objection handling one-pager
- [ ] Demo video they can embed
- [ ] Case studies they can reference

## Referral Program Playbook

### Referral Reward Structures

| Structure | Example | Best For |
|-----------|---------|----------|
| **Give/Get (two-sided)** | "Give a friend $25 off, get $25 credit" | Balanced incentive for both parties |
| **Tiered rewards** | "Refer 1 → $10, 3 → $50, 10 → $200" | Encouraging volume |
| **Product credit** | "Refer → 1 month free" | SaaS, subscription businesses |
| **Cash** | "Refer → $50 cash per signup" | Maximum motivation |
| **Feature unlock** | "Refer 3 friends → unlock premium features" | Product-led growth |

### The AARRR Referral Loop

```
ACTIVATION: Customer has their first "aha" moment
    ↓
TRIGGER: Prompt to refer at moment of highest satisfaction
    ↓
SHARE: Easy sharing mechanism (link, code, one-click invite)
    ↓
INCENTIVE: Clear reward for both referrer and referred
    ↓
ATTRIBUTION: Track who referred whom (unique links/codes)
    ↓
REWARD: Deliver reward immediately (delay kills trust)
    ↓
REINFORCE: Show referral progress ("You've earned $75!")
```

### Referral Timing

**When to ask for referrals (ranked by conversion):**
1. **After first success** — "You just saved 4 hours this week! Know anyone else who would benefit?"
2. **After positive review/NPS** — "Thanks for the 9/10! Would you refer a colleague?"
3. **At renewal/upgrade** — "You're a power user — share with your network for rewards"
4. **Never:** During onboarding, after a support ticket, or when the customer is frustrated

### Referral Program Checklist

- [ ] Two-sided reward defined (referrer + referred both benefit)
- [ ] Sharing mechanism is 1-click (pre-filled message + unique link)
- [ ] Tracking works across devices and sessions
- [ ] Reward delivered within 24 hours of qualifying action
- [ ] Dashboard shows referral count, pending rewards, earned total
- [ ] Email notification when referral converts
- [ ] Anti-fraud measures (IP check, email domain check, minimum account age)
- [ ] Terms and conditions accessible
- [ ] Referral prompt embedded at natural touchpoints (post-purchase, in-app, email)

## Tracking & Attribution

### Essential Tracking Setup

| Method | How | Pro | Con |
|--------|-----|-----|-----|
| UTM links | Unique URL per affiliate | Simple, works everywhere | Can be stripped/replaced |
| Coupon codes | Unique code per affiliate | Easy attribution | Can be shared/leaked |
| Cookie-based | Tracking pixel + cookie | Automatic, multi-session | Cookie blockers, cross-device issues |
| Server-to-server | Postback URL on conversion | Most reliable | Requires technical setup |

### Fraud Prevention

| Fraud Type | Signal | Prevention |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| Self-referral | Same IP, email domain, device | Block same-household referrals |
| Coupon sites | Affiliate's "content" is just the coupon code | Require content review before approval |
| Cookie stuffing | Affiliate loads tracking pixel without user action | Monitor for unusual conversion patterns |
| Fake accounts | Mass signups that never activate | Only pay commission after activation/purchase |
| Incentivized clicks | "Click this link to enter a giveaway" | Require minimum engagement (not just signup) |

## Metrics

| Metric | Affiliate Program | Referral Program |
|--------|------------------|-----------------|
| Activation rate | % of affiliates who make 1+ sale | % of customers who share referral link |
| Conversion rate | Clicks → purchases through affiliate links | Referral clicks → signups |
| Revenue per affiliate | Total revenue / active affiliates | — |
| Customer LTV (referred) | LTV of affiliate-acquired customers | LTV of referred customers |
| Viral coefficient | — | Referrals per customer (>1 = viral growth) |
| Payback period | Time to recoup affiliate commission | Time to recoup referral reward |

### Benchmarks

| Metric | Average | Top Performers |
|--------|---------|---------------|
| Referral program participation | 2-5% of customers | 10-15% |
| Referral conversion rate | 3-8% | 15-25% |
| Affiliate activation rate | 5-10% of signups | 20-30% |
| Revenue from affiliates (of total) | 5-15% | 25-40% |
| Referred customer LTV | 1.2-1.5x organic | 2x+ organic |
