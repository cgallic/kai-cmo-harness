# Instagram Channel Guide

> **Use when:** Building an Instagram presence for brand awareness, community, lead gen, or DTC sales.

---

- **2B+ monthly active users**, skewing 18-44
- **Reels dominate** — 50%+ of time spent on Reels

- **Shopping integration** — seamless path from content to purchase (DTC)

## Reels Strategy

### The 2-Second Rule
Instagram Reels autoplay. Users decide in 2 seconds whether to keep watching. Your first frame must be:
- Visually interesting (not a title card)
- Text overlay with a hook
- Movement or action (not a static face)

### Reels Script Templates

**Educational (15-30s):**
```
[0-1s]  TEXT OVERLAY: "3 things about [topic] you didn't know"
[1-3s]  CONTEXT: Quick intro to why this matters
[3-12s] POINT 1, POINT 2, POINT 3 (fast cuts between each)
[12-15s] CTA: "Save this for later" or "Follow for more"
```

**Transformation (15-30s):**
```
[0-1s]  BEFORE state (ugly, broken, messy)
[1-3s]  Transition effect (swipe, spin, jump cut)
[3-10s] AFTER state revealed with satisfying reveal
[10-15s] Details and explanation
```

**Talking Head with Hook (30-60s):**
```
[0-2s]  HOOK: Bold claim or question on screen
[2-5s]  "Here's why..." — establish credibility quickly
[5-25s] Main content — 2-3 key points, fast-paced
[25-30s] CTA: "Comment [KEYWORD] if you want the full guide"
```

### Reels Best Practices
- **Vertical 9:16** (1080x1920) — always
- **Text overlays** — 85% watch without sound
- **Trending audio** — use trending sounds even at low volume behind voiceover
- **Captions** — auto-captions or burn in captions
- **First 2 seconds** — hook or lose them
- **Hashtags** — 5-10 relevant hashtags (not 30)
- **Cover image** — choose a frame that looks good on your grid
- **Post timing** — 8-10 AM, 12-1 PM, 7-9 PM (test your audience)
- **Engage in first 30 min** — reply to every comment to boost distribution

## Carousel Strategy

### Why Carousels Win

Carousels have the highest save rate of any Instagram format. Saves are a top algorithm signal. When someone saves your carousel, Instagram assumes it's high-value content and shows it to more people.

### Carousel Templates

**Listicle (10 slides):**
```
Slide 1: Hook — "10 [topic] mistakes costing you [outcome]"
Slides 2-9: One mistake per slide (bold headline + 2-line explanation)
Slide 10: CTA — "Save this → Share with someone who needs it"
```

**Step-by-Step (5-8 slides):**
```
Slide 1: "How to [achieve outcome] in [timeframe]"
Slides 2-7: Step 1, Step 2, etc. (one per slide)
Slide 8: Summary + CTA
```

**Before/After or Data:**
```
Slide 1: The headline stat or transformation
Slides 2-5: The breakdown/evidence
Slide 6: What this means for the reader
Slide 7: How to apply it
Slide 8: CTA
```

### Design Rules
- **Consistent template** — same fonts, colors, layout across slides
- **Large text** — minimum 24pt equivalent (readable on phone)
- **One idea per slide** — don't cram
- **Arrow or "swipe" cue** on slide 1 (some people don't know to swipe)
- **Brand colors** — subtle but present

## Stories Strategy

### Daily Stories Cadence
- **3-7 stories per day** is the sweet spot
- Mix: behind-the-scenes, polls/questions, tips, reposts, personal
- Stories disappear in 24h — lower stakes, higher authenticity
- Use story highlights to create an evergreen "mini website" on your profile

### Interactive Stickers (Algorithm Boosters)
- **Poll** — "Which do you prefer: A or B?" (drives engagement)
- **Question box** — "Ask me anything about [topic]" (generates content ideas)
- **Quiz** — "Test your knowledge: [topic]" (education + engagement)
- **Slider** — "How much do you agree?" (low-friction interaction)
- **Countdown** — for launches and events (reminders)
- **Link** — direct link to any URL (all accounts now have this)

## Instagram SEO

### How Instagram Search Works in 2026
- Instagram indexes captions, alt text, hashtags, and profile text
- Users search topics, not just accounts
- Reels surface in search results like YouTube videos

### SEO Checklist
- [ ] Bio: Clear description with primary keywords
- [ ] Bio: CTA + link (Linktree or direct)
- [ ] Username: include primary keyword if possible
- [ ] Captions: use keywords naturally (not keyword-stuffed)
- [ ] Alt text: write descriptive alt text for every image
- [ ] Hashtags: 5-10 relevant, mix of broad + niche
- [ ] Location tags: for local businesses
- [ ] Reels captions: keyword-rich

## Growth Tactics

### Organic Growth Playbook

1. **Reels consistency** — 5-7 Reels/week, prioritize hooks
2. **Engage before posting** — 15 min of genuine comments on others' content before you post
3. **Collab posts** — share posts with relevant accounts (doubles reach)
4. **DM strategy** — reply to every DM, start conversations with engagers
5. **Cross-pollination** — share Reels as TikToks, YouTube Shorts
6. **Story engagement** — daily polls/questions to train the algorithm you're interactive
7. **Hashtag research** — use Instagram search to find hashtags your audience follows

### DM Sales Framework
```
Step 1: Post Reel/carousel with CTA "Comment [WORD] for the free guide"
Step 2: Auto-DM (via ManyChat or similar) with the guide + follow-up question
Step 3: Manual follow-up for qualified leads
Step 4: Offer/CTA in follow-up DM
```

This converts at 5-15% for warm audiences (much higher than link-in-bio).

## Instagram Ads

### Best Performing Ad Placements (2026)

| Placement | CTR | CPM | Best For |
|-----------|-----|-----|----------|
| Reels | Highest | $8-15 | Cold traffic, awareness |
| Feed | Medium | $10-20 | Retargeting, consideration |
| Stories | Medium | $6-12 | Retargeting, urgency |
| Explore | Lower | $5-10 | Discovery, broad awareness |

### Ad Creative Rules
- Reels ads should look like organic Reels (native > polished)
- Use the same aspect ratios as organic content (9:16 for Reels/Stories, 1:1 for Feed)
- Include captions/text overlays (sound-off default)
- UGC/testimonial format outperforms branded creative for conversion
