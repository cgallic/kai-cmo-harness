## Quick Reference

### Algorithmic Authorship (Top 10 Rules)

1. **Conditions AFTER main clause**: "Do X if Y" not "If Y, do X"
2. **Instructions start with verbs**: "Whip lightly" not "Lightly whip"
3. **Short sentences** - break complex apart
4. **Numeric lists** for steps/methods, **bulleted** for types
5. **Name entities twice** before switching to attributes
6. **Anchor words** connect sequential sentences
7. **Examples follow** every declaration
8. **Bold the ANSWER**, not query-matching terms
9. **No links** in first sentence of paragraphs
10. **Same part of speech** in list items

### Four U's (Content Quality)

Score 1-4 on each. Target: 12+/16

| U | Question |
|---|----------|
| **Unique** | Can only WE write this? |
| **Useful** | Can reader take action? |
| **Ultra-specific** | Are there numbers/examples? |
| **Urgent** | Is there reason to engage today? |

### Perception Engineering (3 Layers)

| Layer | Goal | Key Tactic |
|-------|------|------------|
| **Perception** | Destabilize cached beliefs | Re-index "virtues" as "vices" |
| **Context** | Shift what feels allowed | Genre-shift (Exam → Lab) |
| **Permission** | Remove consequences | Future pacing, double binds |

### QDP/QDH/QDS Decision Framework

| Decision | Criteria |
|----------|----------|
| **New Page (QDP)** | >300 monthly searches, distinct intent |
| **New Heading (QDH)** | 50-300 searches, related to page topic |
| **New Sentence (QDS)** | <50 searches, answer inline |
