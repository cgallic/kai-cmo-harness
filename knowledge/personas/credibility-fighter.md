# Persona: The Credibility Fighter

> **Use when:** Targeting women navigating institutional bias, or marketing professional development, healthcare advocacy, or workplace tools.

## Profile

**Who:** Women navigating professional and medical institutions
**Age:** 25-55
**Role:** Professional, patient, or both
**Frustration:** Systemic minimization requiring constant proof of competence

---

## Core Psychology

Faces systemic minimization. In healthcare, pain dismissed as "anxiety" leading to delayed diagnoses. In workplace, faces "Double Bind" - assertive = "aggressive," kind = "weak."

Carries burden of "Non-Promotable Tasks" (NPTs) - office housework (notes, parties) that uses time but offers no career advancement. Constantly interrupted in meetings, ideas appropriated by male colleagues.

---

## Top 10 Pain Points

1. **Medical Gaslighting** - Goes to ER with severe abdominal pain. Asked if "stressed" or "on period." Waits hours longer than male patients. Turns out to be burst appendix.

2. **Office Housework Trap** - In peer meeting, she's asked to "take notes" or "order lunch." Say yes = secretary. Say no = "not a team player." 5 hours/week stolen from promotable work.

3. **Double Bind of Leadership** - Gives direct feedback, called "abrasive" or "bossy." Male colleague gives exact same feedback, called "decisive" and "strong leader."

4. **Hepeating** - Suggests idea in meeting, ignored. Five minutes later, man suggests exact same idea. Praised for brilliance. Must decide: speak up (look petty) or stay silent.

5. **Interrupt Ratio** - Can't finish sentence without interruption. Tracks it: men interrupt women 3x more than they interrupt other men.

6. **Tone Policing** - Performance review feedback about "tone" or "personality" rather than metrics. Told to "smile more" or be "softer."

7. **Diversity Hire Whisper** - Gets earned promotion. Hears whispers it was a "quota" fill. Competence constantly audited by less qualified people.

8. **The Pain Gap** - Denied pain medication for procedure involving cutting internal organs because "the cervix has no nerve endings" (medical lie). Expected to endure silently.

9. **Maternal Wall** - Announces pregnancy, immediately cut from long-term projects. Commitment questioned before leaving.

10. **Emotional Labor Dump** - Expected to remember birthdays, organize sick colleague card, manage team's emotional climate. Not in job description, no credit given.

---

## "This Feels Rigged Because..."

- **The Authority Gap:** Needs 30% more evidence to be believed 50% as much as a man
- **Invisible KPIs:** Judged on criteria (likability, warmth) not applied to men
- **Systemic Minimization:** Institutions (medical, corporate) designed to minimize her voice as default
