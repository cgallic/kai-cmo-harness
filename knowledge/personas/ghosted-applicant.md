# Persona: The Ghosted Applicant

> **Use when:** Targeting job seekers, or marketing career services, resume tools, or recruiting platforms.

## Profile

**Who:** Job seekers and career switchers
**Age:** 22-55
**Role:** Actively searching or passively looking
**Frustration:** Navigating a rigged job market that harvests their labor

---

## Core Psychology

The job market has become a **"digital graveyard"** where applications vanish into the void. Key distortions:
- ~20% of job postings are "Ghost Jobs" with no intent to hire
- ATS systems create perception of algorithmic rejection
- "Brewing" candidates - endless interview loops or unpaid assignments

The job seeker isn't just fighting for a job - they're fighting a system that harvests their data and labor for free.

---

## Top 10 Pain Points

1. **Free Consulting Assignment** - Three interview rounds, then asked to create full 12-month marketing strategy. Weekend spent, ghosted, sees their strategy implemented 3 months later.

2. **Ghost Job Loop** - Applies to "Posted 2 Days Ago" job on LinkedIn. Job has been open 6 months with zero budget, exists only for investor optics.

3. **One-Way Video Interview** - Records answers to generic questions for AI to analyze "micro-expressions." Zero human interaction, deeply dehumanizing.

4. **Entry Level Expert** - "Entry Level" posting requires 5+ years experience in software that's existed for 3 years. Pay: $45k.

5. **ATS Black Box** - Hours tailoring resume with keywords. Auto-rejected in 2 minutes because PDF header broke the parser.

6. **Reposted Rejection** - Gets rejection email saying "went with another candidate." Next morning, same job reposted on LinkedIn.

7. **Unpaid Trial Shift** - "Working interview" to assess culture fit. Works full 8-hour shift for free. Never called back.

8. **Bait and Switch Remote** - Job listed as "Remote." Third interview reveals it's "Hybrid" meaning 4 days/week in office 2 hours away.

9. **Salary Range Gaslight** - Listing says $100k-$150k. Final offer: $95k because "based on your experience, you'd be at the lower end."

10. **Reference Fatigue** - Asked for 3 professional references before phone screen. Burns social capital for 1% chance job.

---

## "This Feels Rigged Because..."

- **Asymmetry of Effort:** Applicant customizes every resume (hours). Company auto-rejects in milliseconds (zero effort).
- **Data Harvesting:** Job listing might be fake, existing solely to harvest resumes for "talent pipeline" or sell data.
- **The Hidden Market:** Significant % of jobs filled via networking/referrals. "Apply Now" button connects to nothing.
- **Internal Hires:** Six interview rounds only to learn company always intended to promote internal candidate.

- "Why 20% of Job Posts Are Designed to Waste Your Time"
- "The $0 Consulting You're Giving Away in Interviews"
- "What Recruiters Won't Tell You About ATS Systems"

### Opening Lines
- "You're not unemployable. The market is literally rigged."
- "That perfect job you applied to? It might not exist."
- "The company that ghosted you is using your strategy right now."

### Validation Angle
Universal trauma. Showing the specific mechanics of exploitation (unpaid assignment, ghost job stats) validates the paranoia that **"it's not you, it's the market."** Exposes the corporate lie that "merit" gets you hired.

## Why This Persona Converts

- **Highly motivated** to find solutions
- **Willing to pay** for legitimate advantage
- **Active engagement** with career content
- **High emotional investment** - solutions feel like lifelines
