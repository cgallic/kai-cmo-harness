# Persona: The Admin Martyr

> **Use when:** Targeting people overwhelmed by life administration, or marketing productivity tools, automation services, or personal finance apps.

## Profile

**Who:** Adults drowning in household and life administration
**Age:** 28-55
**Role:** De facto "Chief Household Officer"
**Frustration:** Death by a thousand administrative tasks

---

## Core Psychology

This persona deals with **"Life Admin"** - the office work of life. Average adult handles 100+ life admin tasks per year, but for this persona, cognitive load is crushing.

Suffer from "Zeigarnik Effect" - brain's tendency to remember unfinished tasks creates constant anxiety hum.

Often the "Default Parent" or partner absorbing administrative debris. Technology promised to simplify but multiplied accounts, portals, and logins required to function.

---

## Top 10 Pain Points

1. **Password Reset Loop** - Needs to pay bill. Forgot password. Reset email takes 10 minutes. Session times out. Gives up, pays late fee next month.

2. **Two-Factor Dance** - Tries to log into utility account. Code sent to partner's phone. Partner in meeting. Can't pay water bill.

3. **Chatbot Gatekeeper** - Has simple question ("Why is bill higher?"). Chatbot refuses human connection, forces irrelevant options until screaming "AGENT" into void.

4. **Paperless Paradox** - Opted for paperless to "save trees." Now has no physical reminder to pay. Notification email buried under 50 marketing emails from same company.

5. **Contract Rollover Trap** - Broadband contract ends. Price automatically doubles. No notification. Must spend 2 hours threatening to leave to get original price.

6. **Scheduling Tetris** - Finding time when plumber can come (8am-5pm window) that doesn't conflict with work meetings. Uses PTO to wait for repair person who might not show.

7. **Return Logistics** - Online clothes don't fit. Returning requires printer (broken) and driving to specific drop-off only open during work hours.

8. **Tax Anxiety** - Has side hustle. Tax rules incomprehensible. Probably overpaying or underpaying. Hiring accountant costs more than discrepancy.

9. **Mental Load Inventory** - Only one who knows when dog needs shots, car registration expires, kids' shoe sizes. If hit by bus, household infrastructure collapses.

10. **Subscription Audit Mystery** - Auditing bank statement. See "AMZN MKTP US" for $14.99. No idea what it is. Investigating takes more energy than losing the $15.

---

## "This Feels Rigged Because..."

- **Friction as Feature:** Easy to buy (1-click), hard to manage (call us)
- **The Pink Tax on Time:** Women disproportionately carry this load. Invisible labor never thanked, only noticed when it fails.
- **Digital Clutter:** Tools meant to "organize" (apps, emails) have become the source of work
