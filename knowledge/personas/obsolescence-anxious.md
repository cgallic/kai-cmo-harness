# Persona: The Obsolescence Anxious

> **Use when:** Targeting mid-career professionals worried about AI/tech displacement, or marketing upskilling platforms, career services, or AI tools.

## Profile

**Who:** Mid-career professionals feeling the ground shift
**Age:** 35-55
**Role:** Established in field, now questioning relevance
**Frustration:** "AI won't replace you, but a person using AI will"

---

## Core Psychology

Dealing with psychological fallout of AI boom. Core anxiety: need to constantly upskill just to maintain current standing - a "Prompt Engineering Rat Race."

Also face "cultural fit" ageism. Job descriptions use coded language ("digital native," "high energy") to filter older workers. See devaluation of institutional knowledge in favor of "speed" and "adaptability."

They are the "Legacy" employees in systems that worship "Next Big Thing."

---

## Top 10 Pain Points

1. **AI Imposter Syndrome** - Uses ChatGPT, realizes it can write in 10 seconds what took them 30 minutes. Cold dread that "soft skills" aren't enough to save them.

2. **Tech Savvy Ageism** - Job ads asking for "Digital Natives" or "High Energy." Knows this is code for "Under 30 and cheap." Dyes gray hair before Zoom interviews.

3. **Prompt Engineering Rat Race** - Told they need "Prompt Engineering" to survive. Spends weekends on YouTube tutorials. AI model updates, prompts no longer work.

4. **The Junior Gap** - AI does entry-level work, so no juniors to mentor. Stuck doing grunt work AND high-level strategy because talent pipeline broken.

5. **Layoff Survivor Guilt** - Team cut in half, workload unchanged. Using AI not to "innovate" but to keep up with impossible volume, hoping no one notices.

6. **New Tool Fatigue** - Every week a "game-changing" tool they MUST learn. More time managing tools than doing work.

7. **Consultant Gaslight** - External consultants (usually younger) tell leadership "AI can replace 50% of headcount." They know AI hallucinates, but leadership only sees cost savings.

8. **LinkedIn Performative Hustle** - Peers posting about building apps "in 4 minutes with AI." Feels like drowning while everyone else speedboats.

9. **Legacy Trap** - Expert in system company wants to kill. Too valuable to fire, too specialized to promote. Stuck maintaining old while excluded from new.

10. **Learning ROI** - Spent $50k on degree now irrelevant. Still paying loan while 19-year-olds on TikTok make more teaching "AI Hustles."

---

## "This Feels Rigged Because..."

- **Moving Goalposts:** Definition of "competence" changes faster than neuroplasticity allows
- **Devaluation of Experience:** "Wisdom" and "institutional knowledge" valued at zero; "speed" and "adaptability" at infinity
- **The Efficiency Trap:** AI makes them more efficient, company just gives more work. Captures all value of increased productivity.
