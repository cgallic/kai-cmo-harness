# Persona Index

> **Use when:** You need to quickly identify the right audience persona for content, messaging, or campaign targeting.

## Core Theme: Transfer of Institutional Burden

All personas share a common thread: **institutions have offloaded labor onto individuals**.

- Corporations offload compliance onto workers (Competent Cog)
- Companies offload service labor onto customers (Subscription Serf)
- Employers offload hiring costs onto applicants (Ghosted Applicant)
- Systems offload childcare burden onto parents (System Manager)

**Content that resonates:** Validates that the difficulty is systemic, not personal failure.

## Using Personas in Content

### Headlines
Use persona-specific pain points:
```
Competent Cog: "Why Your Best Ideas Die in the Approval Queue"
Subscription Serf: "The $40 Fee They're Betting You Won't Fight"
Ghosted Applicant: "The Job Listing That Was Never Real"
```

### Hooks
Lead with "rigged system" validation:
```
"You're not bad at your job. Your job is designed to make you feel bad."
"It's not you. The market is literally rigged."
"You're not crazy. This is happening to everyone."
```

### CTAs
Position your solution as the "hack" or "shortcut":
```
"Skip the bureaucracy with..."
"The cheat code for..."
"What [persona] already know about..."
```
