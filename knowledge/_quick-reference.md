Score content 1-4 on each dimension. Target: 12+/16

| U | Question |
|---|----------|
| **Unique** | Can only WE write this? |
| **Useful** | Can reader take action? |
| **Ultra-specific** | Are there numbers/examples? |
| **Urgent** | Is there reason to engage today? |

## Algorithmic Authorship (Top 10 Rules)

1. Conditions AFTER main clause: "Do X if Y"
2. Instructions start with verbs: "Whip lightly"
3. Short sentences - break complex apart
4. Numeric lists for steps, bulleted for types
5. Name entities twice before attributes
6. Anchor words connect sentences
7. Examples follow every declaration
8. Bold the ANSWER, not query terms
9. No links in first sentence of paragraphs
10. Integrate sources in-text (not footnotes)

## Perception Engineering (3 Layers)

| Layer | Goal | Key Tactic |
|-------|------|------------|
| **Perception** | Destabilize cached beliefs | Re-index "virtues" as "vices" |
| **Context** | Shift what feels allowed | Genre-shift (Exam → Lab) |
| **Permission** | Remove consequences | Future pacing, double binds |

**Key Insight:** Decisions are "installed" by linguistic environments, not "made" by rational evaluation. The brain minimizes cognitive effort via cached predictions.

**Advanced Concepts:** See `perception-engineering-advanced.md` for Active Inference theory, Semiocapitalism, and LLMs as perception machines.

## TikTok Algorithm Essentials

**Signal Hierarchy:**
- Rewatch (~5) > Completion (~4) > Share (~3) > Comment (~2) > Like (~1)

**3-Second Rule:**
- 0-0.7s: Visual interrupt (must work on MUTE)
- 0.7-2.0s: Clear value proposition
- 2.0-3.0s: Curiosity gap / commitment

**Critical Thresholds:**
- 65%+ 3-second retention = 4-7x impressions
- 70%+ completion rate = distribution expansion
- First 60 minutes = peak freshness window

**Suppression Triggers:**
- Watermarks from other platforms
- >5 hashtags
- Low technical quality
- Misleading captions

## B2C Fintech Design Essentials

**Philosophy:** "Streetwearification" of finance - money is identity

**Three Pillars:**
| Pillar | Studio | Focus |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Vibe & Culture | Family NYC | Streetwear aesthetics, 40-120pt numerals |
| Invisible Utility | Work & Co | Optimistic UI, skeleton screens, zero friction |
| Game Feel | Ustwo | Haptics, sonic branding, spring physics |

**Key Patterns:**
- Dark mode default (OLED optimized)
- Swipe-to-pay (positive friction, no "Are you sure?" modals)
- Custom haptic vocabulary (Heavy for big transfers, Ratchet for selection)
- Payment success = full-screen celebration + sound + haptic

**Typography:** CashMarket or extended fonts, 40-120pt for balances ("financial brutalism")

**Anti-Patterns:** No spinners, no Trust Blue, no stock photos, no legal-speak

## Paid Acquisition Essentials

**Unit Economics Benchmarks (B2B SaaS):**
- CAC Payback: <12 months (healthy)
- LTV:CAC: >3:1 (minimum viable)
- Magic Number: >0.75 (efficient growth)

**7-Hook Framework:**
1. Curiosity Gap: "The thing nobody tells you about X"
2. Fear: "The hidden cost of ignoring X"
3. Transformation: "How we went from A to B"
4. Us vs. Them: "X is dead. Here's what's next"
5. Specificity: "The exact framework we used"
6. Story: "We almost gave up, then..."
7. Pattern Interrupt: Unexpected visual/angle

**Platform Quick Wins:**
- Google: Hagakure/STAG structure, Performance Max
- Meta: Advantage+ Shopping, broad targeting + signals
- TikTok: Spark Ads, native-first creative
- LinkedIn: Thought Leader Ads, content funnel

**Measurement Triangulation:**
- MTA (Multi-Touch Attribution): Day-to-day optimization
- MMM (Marketing Mix Modeling): Strategic allocation
- Incrementality Testing: True causal impact

**Anti-Patterns:**
- ROAS Illusion → Optimizing vanity over incrementality
- Over-Segmentation → Fighting algorithm learning
- Set-and-Forget → Creative fatigue decay

## Business Model Quick Reference

**North Stars by Model:**
| Model | North Star | Critical Constraint |
|-------|------------|---------------------|
| **B2B SaaS** | Pipeline Velocity | MQL-to-SQL handoff |
| **DTC** | 60-Day LTV | Rising CAC |
| **Fintech** | Activation Rate | Trust Deficit |
| **Crypto** | Active Wallets | Decentralization vs Execution |
| **Marketplace** | Match Rate | Cold Start |

**Universal Benchmarks:**
- LTV:CAC > 3:1 (all models)
- CAC Payback < 12-18 months

**B2B SaaS Funnel:**
- Visitor → Lead: 2-5%
- MQL → SQL: 15-21% (best: 40%)
- SQL → Opp: 36-42%
- Opp → Close: 20-30%

**DTC Retention:**
- Repeat Purchase: 25-30%
- Email Revenue: 20-30% of total

**Fintech CAC:**
- Consumer Viral: $5-15
- B2B Enterprise: ~$1,450

**Marketplace Liquidity:**
- Match Rate > 60% or churn accelerates

**Team by Stage:**
- Seed: 1 Full Stack or Founder-led
- Series A: Director + 3 specialists
- Series B: VP + Directors + dedicated teams
