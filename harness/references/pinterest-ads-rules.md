# Pinterest Ads — Harness Rules

> Last updated: March 2026. Based on Pinterest's official Advertising Guidelines, Merchant Guidelines, and Community Guidelines, including the 2025 GLP-1 medication policy update.

---

## 1. Prohibited Content (Complete Banned List)

### Adult & Nudity Content
- Sex toys and body-part-shaped products
- Adult/pornographic videos
- Sexual enhancement products
- Escort services
- Imagery implying sexual activity
- Sexual objectification
- Any nudity or implied nudity (blurred images)
- Focus on genitals, pubic hair, buttocks, or female breasts
- Minors in underwear or swimsuits without adults present

### Clickbait
- Sensational clickbait tactics exploiting user curiosity
- Fear-based messaging
- Deficiency critiques ("you're missing out on...")
- Alarmist language
- Withheld information tactics

### Drugs & Paraphernalia
- Illegal and recreational drugs
- Instructional drug content
- Drug imagery
- Drug paraphernalia
- CBD products prohibited EXCEPT: topical hemp seed oil (US only, negligible THC, no therapeutic claims)

### Endangered Species & Live Animals
- Live animal sales
- Products from: tortoise shell, ivory, coral, crocodile skin, pangolin, tiger, polar bear, sea otter
- Medicinal products from rhinos, tigers, or Asiatic black bears

### Illegal Products & Services
- Products facilitating illegal activities
- Software tampering tools
- Malicious links
- Illegal streaming/downloading services
- Fake IDs and fraudulent documents

### Counterfeit Goods
- Knockoffs, brand replicas, goods misrepresented as authentic

### Political Campaigning
- Election ads
- Candidate fundraising
- Political parties
- Issue-based election ads
- Legislation and referendums
- Political merchandise

### Sensitive Content
- Divisive or disturbing advertisements
- Offensive language
- Excessive violence
- Conspiracy theories
- Misinformation
- Election interference content
- Climate denial
- Tragic event exploitation
- Sensitive health content (exception: educational or charitable context)

### Tobacco
- Cigarettes, e-cigarettes, vapes
- Chewing tobacco
- Tobacco paraphernalia
- Nicotine products
- Associated tobacco brands
- Hookahs, cigars, and related products

### Unacceptable Business Practices
- Penny auctions
- Payday loans
- Plaintiff recruitment
- Bail bonds
- Most cryptocurrency (exception: registered/licensed exchanges and wallets)
- Multi-level marketing (MLM) schemes
- Get-rich-quick offers
- Fake tech support
- Hidden costs
- Essay writing services
- Fake follower/engagement sellers
- Negative option billing
- Unrealistic claims
- Deceptive product swaps

### Weapons & Explosives
- Guns and firearms
- Ammunition
- BB guns, airsoft guns, paintball guns
- Fake firearms
- Gun accessories
- Violent-use knives
- Crossbows
- Tasers and stun guns
- Pepper spray
- Fireworks and explosives
- Weapon-making instructions

## 2. Restricted Content (Allowed with Conditions)

### Alcohol
- Allowed with compliance to federal and local laws
- Age-gating required
- Cannot target minors
- Cannot depict underage consumption
- Cannot associate alcohol with:
  - Enhanced performance
  - Social standing
  - Sexual success
- Cannot encourage excessive drinking
- Cannot show intoxication
- Cannot link to risky activities (driving, operating machinery)
- Cannot emphasize high alcohol content
- Geo-targeting restrictions across 50+ territories
- Prohibited entirely in: India, Indonesia, Saudi Arabia, and others (see country-specific section)

### Contests & Sweepstakes
- Cannot require specific image saves
- Cannot require multiple entries via Pinterest actions
- Cannot require payment participation (unless country-specific exceptions)
- Must clarify contest status
- Must avoid suggesting Pinterest sponsorship

### Financial Products & Services
- Must disclose all terms: APR, repayment, fees, penalties
- Cryptocurrency exchanges and wallets allowed if properly registered and licensed
- No financial return promises for crypto
- Minimal incentives permitted (e.g., "$25 bitcoin sign-up bonus")
- Payday loans: prohibited
- Penny auctions: prohibited
- Get-rich-quick: prohibited

### Gambling
- Lotteries: prohibited
- Gambling apps and websites: prohibited
- Fantasy sports with entry fees: prohibited
- Brick-and-mortar casinos: allowed with country restrictions
- Government/charity lotteries: allowed with prior Pinterest approval
- Japan: no charity lotteries; government lotteries require prior approval

### Healthcare
**Prohibited:**
- Clinical trials
- Paternity tests
- Genetic tests (exception: genealogy services)
- Surrogacy services
- Disease prevention or cure claims
- Unsafe healthcare products
- Invasive procedures
- Human body part sales
- Prescription medicines (exception: GLP-1 medications, see below)

**Allowed:**
- Eyeglasses
- Class I and Class II medical devices
- OTC non-prescription medicines (market-specific restrictions)
- OTC contraceptives
- Telehealth services (with restrictions; cannot use lead ads)

### Weight Loss Products & Services (Pinterest Is Notably Strict)
**Prohibited:**
- Body shaming (imagery or language mocking/discrediting certain body types)
- Weight loss pills and supplements
- Worn or applied weight loss products (wraps, patches, creams)
- Before-and-after weight loss imagery
- Liposuction advertising
- Unrealistic cosmetic procedure claims
- Weight loss testimonials
- BMI references
- Language or imagery idealizing certain body types

**Exception (added February 2025):**
- Approved US and Canada healthcare advertisers may promote prescription GLP-1 weight loss medication with prior Pinterest permission

**Still Allowed:**
- Healthy lifestyle, habit, or fitness services and products (if they do not focus on weight loss)

## 3. Creative Requirements

### Image Quality Standards
- Minimum resolution: 600 x 900 pixels
- No fuzzy, blurry, or grainy images
- No unreadable text
- No incorrect orientation
- No poor cropping
- No overly busy layouts (maximum 4 frames, 2 font styles/colors)
- No artificial functionality buttons (fake play buttons, fake interactive elements)
- No grammar errors in overlaid text
- No excessive capitalization
- No low-quality user-generated content
- No flashing or strobing effects
- No watermarked images
- No pixelated images

### Text Overlay
- Pinterest does not publish a strict text percentage rule
- Minimal overlays preferred; short phrases work best
- Crowded or sales-heavy images reduce approval rates and performance
- Avoid excessive promotional text

### Before-and-After Imagery
- Before-and-after comparisons trigger rejections
- Especially strict for weight loss, cosmetic procedures, and health products

### Landing Page Quality
- Pages cannot require signup before content access (unless clearly stated upfront)
- No pop-ups
- No forced page visits
- Cannot be primarily advertising content
- Must deliver consistent experience matching the ad
- Ad image, description, and landing page must align consistently
- Product or service must be clearly displayed
- Price must match between ad and landing page

### Ad Format Specs

**Standard Pins:**
- Recommended aspect ratio: 2:3 (1000 x 1500 px)
- Minimum: 600 x 900 px
- JPG or PNG

**Video Pins:**
- Aspect ratios: 1:1 (square), 2:3 (vertical), 9:16 (max vertical)
- Min resolution: 240p
- Max file size: 2GB
- Duration: 4 seconds to 15 minutes

**Carousel Ads:**
- 2-5 cards
- Each card: same specs as standard pins
- Each card can have its own title, description, and landing page

**Shopping Pins:**
- Must pull from product feed
- Image must match the product on the landing page
- Price must match

**Collections Ads:**
- Hero image/video + 3 secondary images
- All must link to product pages

## 4. Shopping Ads / Merchant-Specific Rules

### Merchant Requirements
- Cannot be affiliate marketers or wholesale retailers (exception: resale/consignment limited to Ads distribution)
- Must maintain high-quality website with complete product pages
- Must have easily accessible, clear return policy (acceptance status, process, timeframes, contact info)
- Must have clear shipping policy
- Must have easy-to-find contact details
- No forced registration on website
- Safe, non-deceptive website design required

### Product Feed Requirements

**7 Mandatory Fields:**
1. **Title**: must match product name on landing page; include variation details (color, size)
2. **Description**: no HTML formatting; plain text only
3. **Link**: must point directly to the same product and display matching data
4. **ID**: unique product identifier
5. **Image_link**: minimum 75 x 75 pixels (recommended much larger)
6. **Price**: actual product cost; include ISO-4217 currency code if not USD
7. **Availability**: must accurately reflect current stock status

### Feed Accuracy
- Pricing and stock information must be current within seven days
- Daily feed syncing or meta-tag scraping required
- Products must be specific items available for purchase (no services)
- All products must comply with community guidelines and terms of service

### Image Standards for Shopping
- No low-res, pixelated, or watermarked images
- Prices on Pinterest must match prices on site
- Cannot show products as "in stock" if they are not
- Product page must be live, shoppable, and match the product shown on Pinterest

### Discount & Promotion Rules
- Discounts cannot exceed 90% off
- Conditions must be clearly visible
- Promotions must end when expired
- Must comply with local regulations
- Custom promotions require grammatically correct, non-offensive language
- Avoid sensitive category terms in promotional language
- Featured products must match advertised discounts

### Vehicle Catalogs
- Restricted to Canada, Germany, and US only

### Enforcement
- Pinterest reserves rights to remove product pins
- Deny feed access based on poor ratings
- Terminate merchant accounts for guideline violations

## 5. Body Image / Weight Loss Policy (Detailed)

Pinterest was the first major social media platform to ban weight loss ads (July 2021). The policy has been progressively strengthened.

### Completely Prohibited
- Body shaming imagery or language that mocks or discredits certain body types
- Weight loss pills and supplements
- Products claiming weight loss through something worn or applied to the skin
- Before-and-after weight loss imagery
- Weight loss testimonials (exception: approved GLP-1 ads)
- BMI references
- Language idealizing certain body types
- Liposuction and similar invasive procedures for cosmetic weight loss
- Unrealistic cosmetic claims
- Weight loss supplements of any kind

### February 2025 GLP-1 Exception
- Approved US and Canada healthcare advertisers only
- Must be advertising prescription GLP-1 medications specifically
- Requires prior permission from Pinterest
- Does not open the door to general weight loss advertising

### What IS Allowed
- Healthy lifestyle content
- Fitness services and products
- Nutrition-focused content
- Wellness products
- Key requirement: content must NOT focus on weight loss as the primary message

## 6. Targeting Restrictions

### Sensitive Category Targeting (Prohibited)
Cannot target based on:
- Sensitive health or medical conditions
- Race, color, or ethnic origin
- Personal hardship
- Religious or philosophical beliefs
- Sexual behavior, orientation, or gender identity
- Crime commission
- Trade union membership
- Political affiliation
- Proxy targeting for sensitive categories also prohibited

### Minor Protection
- No ads may target minors under 18 for any category

### Protected Group Targeting
- Credit, employment, and housing ads cannot use:
  - Actalike (lookalike) targeting
  - Age targeting
  - Gender targeting
  - Postal code targeting (in certain markets)
- Cannot exclude protected groups

### Data Collection Rules
- Must disclose and obtain legally-required consent for all data collection
- Pinterest tag users must be informed of third-party sharing and AdChoices opt-out options
- Cannot collect sensitive category data
- Cannot collect child data or data from those under 13/age of consent
- User identifiers only in designated fields

### Data Usage Restrictions
- Collected data can only be used for understanding Pinterest campaigns (anonymous basis)
- Cannot share with third parties
- Cannot join with personally identifiable information
- Data tied to unique identifiers must be deleted within 6 months
- Cannot be used for marketing purposes

### Lead Ad Restrictions
- Pharmacy, OTC products, health apps, and telehealth services cannot use lead ads
- Collect only legitimate, necessary data respecting minimization principles
- Example violation: asking for both date of birth AND age (age calculable from DOB)

**Cannot request via lead ads:**
- Sensitive health conditions
- Race/ethnicity
- Personal hardship
- Religious beliefs
- Sexual behavior
- Crime-related info
- Trade union membership
- Political affiliation
- Minors' data
- Precise geolocation
- Account numbers
- Financial information
- Government IDs
- Insurance info
- Passwords
- Citizenship status
- Marital/relationship status
- Military status

## 7. Common Rejection Reasons

### Most Frequent Causes
1. **Excessive text on images** — too much promotional text overlaid on pin images
2. **False or exaggerated product claims** — misleading benefits or unrealistic outcomes
3. **Before-and-after comparisons** — especially health, beauty, and weight-related
4. **Landing page mismatch** — ad content does not match destination page
5. **Weight loss content** — any ad that focuses on weight loss triggers rejection
6. **Body shaming imagery/language** — mocking or idealizing certain body types
7. **Clickbait tactics** — sensationalized headlines, withheld information
8. **Adult or suggestive content** — even mild nudity or sexual posing gets flagged
9. **Low-quality images** — blurry, pixelated, or watermarked images
10. **Prohibited product categories** — weapons, drugs, tobacco, counterfeit goods
11. **Price inconsistency** — ad price doesn't match landing page price
12. **Product availability** — showing "in stock" for out-of-stock items
13. **Missing merchant policies** — no return policy, shipping info, or contact details
14. **Dropshipping quality** — low-quality dropshipping pages frequently fail approval

### Review Process
- Pinterest's team manually reviews some ads before display
- Some ads are reviewed automatically
- Platform reviews the complete user journey, not just individual pins
- Affiliate and dropshipping content faces higher scrutiny

## 8. Country-Specific Restrictions

### Alcohol Restrictions by Country
- **Prohibited entirely**: India, Indonesia, Saudi Arabia, and many others
- **Restricted with conditions**: Most Western markets require age-gating and local law compliance
- Geo-targeting restrictions across 50+ territories

### Healthcare by Country
- **India**: alcohol, casinos, healthcare services, OTC drugs, pre-natal sex determination, infant formula prohibited
- **Indonesia**: alcohol, infant formula, casinos, OTC drugs prohibited
- **Saudi Arabia**: alcohol and casinos prohibited; financial products need licensing
- **Canada**: prior Pinterest approval needed for prescription GLP-1 medication ads; specific pharma restrictions
- **France**: ads must be in French; infant formula under 4 months prohibited
- **Brazil**: ads must be in Portuguese; infant formula under 12 months prohibited
- **Poland**: ads must be in Polish; casinos, infant formula prohibited
- **Japan**: no charity lotteries; government lotteries require prior approval
- **Albania**: infant formula, baby food, bottles, pacifiers, supplements, condoms prohibited for shopping ads

### Language Requirements
- Several countries require ads to be in the local language (France, Brazil, Poland, etc.)

## 9. Recent Policy Changes (2025-2026)

### February 2025
- **GLP-1 Weight Loss Medication Exception**: Pinterest revised weight loss ad policy to allow ads for prescription and compounded GLP-1 weight loss medications on a restricted basis in the US and Canada. Advertisers must be approved healthcare advertisers with prior Pinterest permission.

### Ongoing Enforcement Trends
- Stricter automated review for before-and-after imagery
- Increased scrutiny on health and wellness claims
- Tighter enforcement on product feed accuracy for shopping ads
- Enhanced body image policy enforcement extending to cosmetic procedure ads
- Greater restriction on sensationalized/clickbait creative

## 10. Affiliate & Dropshipping Rules

- Affiliate marketing as a merchant: prohibited (cannot be an affiliate marketer using Pinterest merchant features)
- Dropshipping: permitted ONLY with trustworthy landing pages featuring:
  - Fast load times
  - Transparent pricing
  - Genuine value propositions
  - Real product photos (not stock images)
- Low-quality dropshipping pages frequently fail approval
- Resale/consignment marketplaces limited to Ads distribution only

## Quality Gate Integration

For the harness quality pipeline, Pinterest ads should pass:
- `banned_word_check.py` — zero Tier 1 violations
- `four_us_score.py` — minimum 10/16 for ads
- Image quality check — minimum 600x900px, no blur/watermarks
- Body image policy check — zero weight loss focus, no before/after, no body shaming
- Clickbait check — no fear-based messaging, alarmist language, or withheld information
- Landing page consistency check — ad content matches destination page
- Price accuracy check — ad price matches landing page price (shopping ads)
- Feed accuracy check — all 7 mandatory fields present and current (shopping ads)
