# LinkedIn Ads — Harness Rules

> Last updated: March 2026. Based on LinkedIn's official Advertising Policies (updated November 2025) and the Professional Trust Initiative (PTI) rollout in 2026.

---

## 1. Prohibited Content (Absolute Bans)

### Illegal Products & Activities
- All illegal goods, services, and activities
- Must comply with all applicable laws including content and targeting regulations

### Discrimination
- No discrimination based on age, gender, gender identity, disability, religion, ethnicity, race, color, national origin, or sexual orientation
- Cannot violate education, housing, credit, or employment discrimination laws

### Offensive Content
- Cannot be hateful, vulgar, sexually suggestive, or violent
- Must maintain professional standards of good taste

### Fraud & Deception
- Products must match ad descriptions accurately
- All claims require factual support
- No deceptive competitive comparisons
- Cannot imply false affiliations or endorsements
- Prices and discounts must be accurate and easily discoverable

### Intellectual Property
- Cannot use third-party trademarks without permission
- Cannot use copyrighted material without authorization
- Cannot use logos or company names in a confusing manner

### Fake Documents
- Ads selling fake or counterfeit documents are prohibited

### Tobacco
- ALL tobacco products banned: cigarettes, pipes, cigars, e-cigarettes, vaporizers, and related equipment
- Exception: tobacco-cessation products are allowed

### Drugs
- Illicit and recreational drug promotion prohibited

### Weapons & Violence
- Weapons, ammunition, fireworks, and violent products/services banned

### Adult Content
- All adult products and services prohibited

### Counterfeit Goods
- All counterfeit merchandise banned

### Gambling & Sweepstakes
- All gambling and sweepstakes prohibited

### Occult Services
- Fortune telling, dream interpretation, individual horoscopes banned
- Exception: entertainment/amusement context only

### Health Claims
- Unrealistic or misleading health, diet, or weight loss claims prohibited
- Cannot promote unsafe behaviors (e.g., excessive consumption)

### Affiliate Advertising
- Affiliate ad promotion banned
- In-stream video ads in sponsored content require prior authorization

### Political Ads
- Political candidate, party, and ballot advocacy banned globally
- Political fundraising prohibited
- Ads exploiting sensitive political issues banned
- Policy advocacy and govtech ads also prohibited
- Brazil: TSE Resolution 23732/2024 compliance required
- EU: Regulation (EU) 2024/900 compliance required

### Sensitive Events
- Cannot run ads during tragedies, disasters, or sensitive events

### Hacking & Circumvention
- Products/services for hacking, cracking, or circumvention prohibited

### Multi-Level Marketing
- All recruiting-focused business opportunity ads prohibited

### Countdown Timers
- Explicitly prohibited on LinkedIn (allowed on Meta/Google)

### Third-Party Disparagement
- Comparative or negative competitor claims prohibited
- "Unlike other solutions" and "tired of tools that don't work" trigger rejection

## 2. Restricted Content (Allowed with Conditions)

### Alcohol
- Only in certain countries
- Cannot target under legal drinking age (minimum age 25 on LinkedIn)
- Requires LinkedIn Ad Sales team approval

### Animal Products
- Subject to LinkedIn discretion
- Endangered species products prohibited

### Dating Services
- Cannot target under 18
- Must be legal in jurisdiction
- No compensation for participation ("escort" services)
- Subject to LinkedIn discretion

### Contests & Competitions
- Must comply with LinkedIn-specific guidelines

### Fund Solicitation
- Tax-deductible or charitable qualification required
- Must comply with applicable laws

### Prescription Drugs
- Requires prior authorization
- US/Canada only
- Cannot target under 18
- Specific compliance rules apply

### Over-the-Counter Drugs
- Requires prior authorization
- Legal jurisdictions only
- Cannot target under 18
- Must follow all applicable regulations

### Pharmacy & Telehealth
- Requires prior authorization
- US only
- Cannot target under 18

### Medical Devices & Treatments
- Legal jurisdictions only
- Must follow all applicable regulations
- Cannot target under 18

### Financial Services
- Restricted categories: lending, mortgages, credit, investments, trading, insurance, pensions
- UK: Financial Conduct Authority (FCA) authorization required
- No forward-looking performance claims or hypothetical returns
- Financial outcome testimonials prohibited ("We implemented their platform and AUM grew 200%")
- Compliant testimonials focus on operations only
- Tiered verification system:
  - Tier 1 (B2B fintech SaaS): Standard review, 24-48 hrs
  - Tier 2 (Banking/lending tech): Financial services verification, 3-5 days
  - Tier 3 (Investment/wealth tech): Regulatory license required, 5-10 days
  - Tier 4 (Cryptocurrency): Case-by-case, 10-20 days, near-total restriction
- Tier 3 ads require jurisdiction-specific disclaimers (EU/ESMA, UK/FCA, US/SEC-FINRA)

### Cryptocurrency
- 95% restricted; enterprise blockchain infrastructure only
- Case-by-case review with 10-20 day approval timeline

### Payday/Short-Term Loans
- Restricted; paycheck advances and similar short-term loan products

## 3. Professional Standards (LinkedIn-Specific)

### Professional Trust Initiative (PTI) — New in 2026
- LinkedIn rejected 43% more ads in Q1 2026 vs 2025
- Every ad treated as reflecting the broader LinkedIn ecosystem
- Compliance directly impacts CPCs, delivery rates, and account health via Account Quality Score
- Pattern rejections can increase CPCs 40-60% above market rates

### Professional Tone Requirement
- NLP classifiers flag consumer-style urgency language
- "Stop Losing Deals!" and "Grab Your Free Trial NOW" trigger rejection
- Must use informational, data-backed positioning vs emotional manipulation

### Member Experience Integrity
- Ads must enhance rather than degrade user experience
- Clickbait headlines with rhetorical questions lacking substantive value trigger rejection

### Professional Relevance Score (PRS) — New in 2026
- Evaluates alignment between targeting, creative, and professional context
- Generic copy receives lower delivery and higher review likelihood

### Sensationalized Language
- "Your competitors are ahead," "you're losing money daily" trigger automatic flags
- No countdown timers

### Emoji Rules
- Maximum 2 emojis per ad unit
- Only professional emojis allowed (checkmarks, arrows)
- "Attention-seeking" emojis (fire, rocket, money bag) flagged automatically

### Text & Editorial Standards
- No spelling or grammar errors
- No excessive punctuation, ALL-CAPS, or unrelated hashtags
- LinkedIn-supported languages only
- 20% text-overlay maximum in images (enforced via automated scanning)

### Imagery Standards
- Stock photography discouraged; preference for original/branded content or product screenshots
- Before-and-after visuals and misleading claims trigger rejections

### Claim Substantiation (Stricter Than Other Platforms)
- Every quantitative claim requires named research source, customer case study, or methodology-documented internal data
- Statistics must include a date or timeframe
- Claims based on data older than 18 months considered misleading
- Customer logos require documented permission (spot-checked by LinkedIn)
- G2/review platform references must cite specific report name and date (within last 2 quarters)

### LinkedIn Trademark Use
- Only "find me on LinkedIn" phrase permitted per trademark guidelines
- Cannot imply LinkedIn affiliation or endorsement

## 4. Targeting Restrictions

### Sensitive Data Targeting (Prohibited)
Cannot target based on:
- Political affiliation
- Racial/ethnic origin
- Health data
- Genetic data
- Biometric data
- Religious beliefs
- Criminal record
- Sexual behavior or orientation
- Union membership
- Income

### Employment, Housing, Credit, Education Ads
- Cannot use age targeting/exclusion
- Cannot use gender-based targeting
- Cannot use ethnicity/race targeting (direct or proxy)
- Cannot use religious targeting
- Cannot use disability-based targeting
- Advertisers must certify non-discrimination before accessing age/gender targeting options
- Recruitment campaign objective: age targeting not available at all

### Recruitment-Specific Rules
- Job titles must be gender-neutral ("Sales Representative" not "Salesman")
- Age-coded language prohibited: "digital native," "recent graduate," "young and dynamic," "seasoned professional"
- "Cultural fit" flagged as potential proxy for discrimination
- Physical requirements limited to bona fide occupational qualifications
- Pay transparency: salary ranges required in recruitment ads targeting Colorado, New York, California, Washington, and EU
- Staffing agencies must clearly identify as recruitment agency, disclose position type (temp, contract, permanent), and have documented authorization for hiring company brand assets

## 5. Ad Format Requirements & Specs

### Single Image Sponsored Content
- Introductory text: 600 characters max (150 recommended to avoid truncation)
- Headline: 200 characters max (70 recommended)
- Image: 1200 x 627 px recommended; supports 1:1, 1.91:1, and 1:1.91 aspect ratios
- Image file: JPG or PNG, up to 5MB
- 20% text-overlay maximum in images
- Headline must accurately describe linked content; "curiosity gap" formulations rejected

### Video Sponsored Content
- Headline: 200 characters max (70 recommended)
- Body: 600 characters max
- First 3 seconds must avoid urgency, sensationalized content, or misleading visuals
- Captions mandatory for accessibility (caption text also policy-reviewed)
- SaaS product videos require actual product footage (screen recordings, UI, dashboards) — no lifestyle imagery
- End cards with CTAs cannot use urgency language

### Carousel Ads
- Introductory text: 255 characters
- Card headline: 45 characters (30 characters with Lead Gen Form CTA)
- 2-10 cards per carousel
- Each card: 1080 x 1080 px (1:1 aspect ratio)
- Each card reviewed independently for policy compliance
- Consistent narrative arc required; cannot pivot abruptly from educational to hard sales
- Tone and value proposition must remain consistent across all cards

### Message Ads (InMail / Sponsored InMail)
- Message body: up to 1,500 characters
- Frequency cap: maximum one Message Ad per member every 45 days
- Cannot circumvent frequency cap with multiple campaigns to same audience
- Sender must be a real LinkedIn member who is an actual company employee
- Fake profiles or inactive employees prohibited as senders
- Members clicking "No thanks" cannot be retargeted for 90 days minimum
- No attachments allowed
- Cannot reference recipient by inferred characteristics ("As a VP of Sales...")
- Must disclose sponsored status in first two message lines

### Text Ads
- Headline: 50 characters max
- CTA button text: 18 characters max
- Company logo/image: 100 x 100 px, up to 2MB, JPG or PNG

### Dynamic Ads
- Headline: 50 characters max
- Description: 70 characters max
- Personalization using member name/photo cannot imply LinkedIn endorsement or pre-existing relationship
- "Your peers at [Company] are using..." compliant if factually accurate
- "We noticed you're struggling..." prohibited

### Conversation Ads
- Every branch/CTA link must independently comply with policies
- Cannot route all branches to generic landing page; context-specific destinations required

### Document Ads
- Supports PDF, DOC, PPT files up to 100MB
- URLs: must have http:// or https://, up to 2,000 characters

## 6. B2B-Specific Rules

### Lead Gen Form Requirements (2026 Update)
LinkedIn treats itself as a joint data controller, imposing requirements exceeding GDPR/CCPA:

**Mandatory Elements:**
- Privacy policy link: must be functioning HTTPS URL that explicitly mentions LinkedIn, describes specific data fields, states legal basis, and provides data subject access/deletion instructions
- Explicit opt-in: pre-checked boxes prohibited; members must actively consent
- Granular consent: separate checkboxes for each processing purpose (marketing vs. sales follow-up)
- Consent recording: maintain records for minimum 3 years; LinkedIn audits large advertisers
- Right to withdraw: disclosure must state how members withdraw consent post-submission

**Data Field Restrictions:**
| Field | Allowed For | Restricted For |
|-------|------------|----------------|
| Work Email | All B2B | N/A |
| Phone Number | Enterprise software, consulting | Content downloads, webinars |
| Company Revenue | Financial services, enterprise | SMB campaigns |
| Custom Questions | All (with manual review) | Questions about protected characteristics |
| Personal Address | Direct mail (rare approval) | Most B2B / digital-only |

**CRM Integration Compliance:**
- Workflows must respect original consent scope (whitepaper downloaders cannot auto-enroll in sales cadences without separate consent)
- Third-party data enrichment permitted only if enrichment source has lawful basis
- Sensitive categories (health, politics, ethnicity) strictly prohibited for enrichment
- Cross-border EU transfers require Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) documentation

### SaaS & Software-Specific Rules

**Claim Substantiation Requirements:**
Every quantitative claim needs documentation LinkedIn can request:
- Performance metrics ("reduces churn by 30%")
- ROI claims ("5x return on investment")
- Time claims ("implement in 2 hours")
- User count claims ("trusted by 10,000+ companies")
- Comparison claims ("fastest platform in category")
- Award/recognition claims ("named Leader by Gartner")

**Acceptable Substantiation:**
- Published case studies (customer name required; anonymized rejected; methodology stated)
- Third-party research (Gartner, Forrester, IDC — dated within 18 months)
- Internal data with methodology documentation (sample size, measurement period, statistical significance)
- Current certifications/awards from recognized bodies

**Free Trial/Freemium Disclosure:**
Must state duration of trial, credit card requirement (yes/no), post-trial outcome (auto-conversion or deactivation), and free tier feature limitations.

**Customer Logo Usage:**
- Requires documented permission from each logo owner
- LinkedIn review team spot-checks permissions for high-visibility ads
- Retroactive suspension possible if authorization absent

**Review Platform References (G2, Capterra, etc.):**
- Must cite specific report ("G2 Winter 2026 Grid Report," not generic "Top Rated")
- Rating must be current (within last 2 quarters)
- Cannot imply platform endorsement
- Badge images require valid G2 license

## 8. Landing Page Requirements

- Content must match ad's specific promise
- LinkedIn crawls destination URLs and performs semantic analysis
- Offered content must be prominently featured above fold
- Display URL domain must match destination URL domain
- All users must be sent to the same destination
- No pop-ups on landing pages
- Users must navigate away freely including via back button
- No dark patterns (hidden terms, fake urgency, misleading badges)
- Required disclaimers and disclosures must be present
- Mobile-responsive with no broken elements
- HTTPS required for sites collecting sensitive information (financial data, government ID, login credentials, minor info)

## 9. Safety & Privacy Requirements

### Malicious Software
- No viruses, worms, spyware, or malware distribution
- Software needs user permission and must be removable
- All features must be disclosed
- Cannot exploit browser flaws

### Phishing
- No deceptive websites collecting personal information

### Privacy Compliance
- Advertisers responsible for all privacy and data protection laws
- No tracking cookies across sites without disclosure and consent
- No "ever cookies" or "zombie cookies"

## 10. Appeal Process

### Two-Tier System
1. **Automated re-review**: 24-48 hours
2. **Human escalation** (if rejected): 3-7 business days

### Best Practices for Appeals
- Be specific about changes made or compliance reasoning
- Attach supporting documentation
- Reference specific policy language
- Maintain professional, factual tone
- Submit Monday-Thursday during business hours

### Account Quality Score
- New metric aggregating rejection history, user feedback, and appeal patterns
- Pattern rejections can increase CPCs 40-60% above market rates
- Account suspension/termination for repeated violations

## 11. Review Process

- Reviews typically within 24 hours
- Rejection reason provided in Campaign Manager (hover) and via email
- Can edit and resubmit
- LinkedIn reserves discretion to reject, approve, or remove any ad at any time
- Policies updated periodically; subject to change without notice
