# Microsoft / Bing Ads — Harness Rules

> Last updated: March 2026. Based on Microsoft Advertising official policies, editorial reason codes, and policy change log through 2025-2026.

---

## 1. Prohibited Content (Complete Banned List)

### Illegal Products & Services
- All illegal goods, services, and activities
- Counterfeit goods, brand replicas, knockoffs
- Products or services facilitating illegal activities
- Products enabling bypassing or deceiving public systems, regulations, procedures, or individuals

### Sexually Explicit Content
- Sexually explicit content not allowed in the general marketplace
- Adult advertising restricted to Adult Marketplace Program participants only
- Escorts, strippers, and other adult services: not allowed even for Adult Marketplace
- Extreme, shocking, or obscene adult content: never allowed
- Adult content featuring animals: never allowed
- Adult content featuring violent, abusive, or forced scenarios: never allowed
- Online sale of R18 adult DVDs: not allowed

### Hate Speech & Violence
- Content targeting individuals or groups based on race, religion, gender, sexual orientation, or other protected characteristics
- Content glorifying or promoting violence, criminal activity, or dangerous behaviors
- Content that glorifies suffering or violence
- Real-world threats of violence targeting individuals, groups, buildings, or infrastructure
- Content promoting sexual services or human trafficking

### Weapons & Ammunition
- Firearms and ammunition prohibited
- Gun parts and hardware prohibited
- Firearm accessories prohibited
- Knives positioned as weapons: switchblade knives, disguised knives, buckle knives, lipstick case knives, air gauge knives, knuckle knives, writing pen knives
- Fireworks (online sale) prohibited

### Tobacco Products
- Cigarettes, cigars, smokeless tobacco, and all tobacco products
- Electronic cigarettes including cartridges and liquid nicotine solutions

### Drugs & Paraphernalia
- Illegal substances and substances whose primary purpose is mind alteration
- Drug paraphernalia
- Promotions or glorification of recreational and illegal drugs

### Misinformation & Deception
- False or misleading information regarding products, services, or current events
- Ad or site content that attempts to mislead site visitors
- Content that can be reasonably perceived as deceptive, fraudulent, or harmful
- Unsupported claims in website or ad text

### Unauthorized Financial Offers
- Get-rich-quick schemes
- Deceptive financial products promising unrealistic returns
- Legally questionable business opportunities
- Penny stocks (banned on Audience Network)

### Political Content (Varies by Market)
- Ads exploiting sensitive political issues
- All political advertising must comply with national, state, and local election laws
- Cannot exploit political agendas for commercial gain
- EU Regulation 2024/900: political advertising declaration requirement (effective October 2025)

### Copyrighted & IP Violations
- Trademark and intellectual property infringement prohibited
- Copyright-infringing images or logos
- File sharing of copyrighted content or platforms enabling such sharing
- Usenet newsgroup access sites

### Other Prohibited Categories
- Bulk-marketing products/services where implied use is unsolicited spam
- Counterfeit goods
- Products from endangered species
- Extreme religious agendas or exploitation of sensitive religious issues
- Technical support for products/services not directly owned
- Data reselling content
- Surveillance equipment for non-legitimate use
- Sites that install, copy, or download programs without express prior consent
- Clinical trials and experimental treatments (global ban, effective August 2025)

### Sensitive Events
- Microsoft reserves the right to remove or limit advertising in response to sensitive tragedies, disasters, deaths, or high-profile news events

## 2. Restricted Content (Conditional Approval Required)

### Alcohol
- Online sale of beer, wine, or spirits must comply with standard industry and regulatory requirements
- Age verification mechanisms required
- Regional compliance with local laws mandatory
- Not allowed in some markets

### Gambling & Betting
- Restricted to specific countries and regions
- Advertisers must be licensed in every market they target
- Must complete gambling enablement process and obtain approval
- **Belgium**: complete gambling ad ban
- **Netherlands**: gambling ads only to users aged 24+
- **Ireland**: ads not allowed between 5:30 AM and 9:00 PM (watershed restriction)
- Gambling prohibited on Audience Network except in policy-approved markets

### Adult Content (Marketplace Program)
- Restricted to advertisers in the Adult Marketplace Program
- Adult-enabled advertisers restricted to terms on the approved Adult Keyword List
- No dynamic insertion into ad copy within Adult Marketplace Program
- Non-adult sites linking to adult content must separate via bridge page
- Adult advertising no longer allowed in Hungary or Poland

### Healthcare & Pharmaceuticals
- Prescription drug ads require certification for each market
- US: requires NABP VIPPS or Vet-VIPPS accreditation, or .Pharmacy TLD
- Canada: requires NABP accreditation or .Pharmacy TLD
- Pharmaceutical sellers must receive LegitScript certification or NABP accreditation
- Cannot create impression of direct health targeting ("your condition," "your symptoms")
- Advertising for health care products must adhere to all applicable regulations
- Cosmetic surgery requires applicable licensing compliance (market-specific)

### Supplements & Health Products
- Vitamins and supplements allowed on Audience Network with restrictions
- Ads cannot claim to complete a task (e.g., "cleanses the liver")
- Claims must be accurate and truthful
- Landing pages must be product pages — no advertorials or videos
- Products posing significant health risks: banned
- Microsoft maintains a specific list of disapproved healthcare products and supplements

### Financial Products & Services
- Must comply with all applicable local laws and regulatory requirements
- Transparency about risks and fees mandatory
- Health or financial claims require certification or verification
- Loans, investments, and similar offerings require local regulatory compliance
- Binary options and high-risk financial instruments: heavily restricted
- Payday lending: restricted with specific regional rules

### Medical Content
- Restricted promotion of medical content
- Healthcare advertising must adhere to all applicable regulations
- Drug and alcohol addiction recovery advertising: requires exception approval

### Dating Services
- Mature dating services: restricted in some markets
- International dating services: some are not allowed

### Contests & Competitions
- Must comply with all applicable legislation

### Body Art / Modification
- Mature body art/modification: restricted in some markets

### Mature Content Categories
- Mature movies: restricted in some markets
- Mature sexual health products: restricted in some markets
- Mature video games: restricted in some markets

### Audience Network Additional Restrictions
The Microsoft Audience Network applies stricter rules than Search:
- Gambling: prohibited except in policy-approved markets
- Lawsuit/plaintiff recruitment: prohibited
- Health supplements: allowed with restrictions (product pages only)
- End-of-life services: prohibited
- Dietary supplements: restricted
- Background checks: restricted
- Downloads: restricted
- Psychic/magic content: prohibited
- Penny stocks: prohibited

### Geographic-Specific Restrictions
- **Brazil, India, Vietnam**: infant feeding products (baby formula, feeding bottles, rubber nipples, baby food) prohibited
- **Belgium**: gambling ads completely banned
- **Hungary, Poland**: adult advertising not allowed

## 3. Editorial Requirements

### Capitalization
- Allowed for proper names, branded words, and legitimate acronyms
- Random or excessive capitalization not allowed
- Example approved: "Purchase Cheddar cheese from England"
- Example disapproved: "Find GREAT deals on soFTwaRe"

### Grammar & Spelling
- Ad title and ad text must be grammatically correct
- Ad title and ad text must be spelled correctly
- Foreign language inappropriate for the target market is not allowed

### Punctuation
- Must be punctuated correctly and not excessively
- Repeated punctuation or symbols not allowed
- Excessive or inappropriate symbols disallowed

### Ad Content Minimums
- Ad title and description must contain three or more words
- Mobile ad text must also contain three or more words

### Promotional Language
- Excessive or inappropriate promotional language not allowed
- Superlatives must be substantiated

### Calls to Action
- Non-specific calls to action not allowed (e.g., "click here")
- Must be descriptive and relevant

### Repetition
- Repetitive words in ad text not allowed

### Style Compliance
- Ad title and ad text must comply with Microsoft's style guidelines
- Phone numbers must be formatted correctly with valid digits
- No disallowed characters in phone numbers
- Phone numbers in ad text restricted

### Display URL
- Must be formatted correctly
- Only one display URL domain allowed per ad group
- Display URL must match the actual landing page domain

## 4. Destination / Landing Page Requirements

### Accessibility
- Website must be accessible at all times
- Repeat inaccessible website failures trigger disapproval

### Content Relevance
- Landing page content must be relevant to keyword and ad text
- Website must contain the offer indicated in the ad text
- Site must be related to ad text
- Sufficient content required (thin content sites rejected)

### User Experience
- No pop-ups or pop-unders when users enter or leave the landing page
- No disallowed animation, audio, or video behaviors
- No functionality that limits user navigation or operates in non-standard manner
- Users must be able to navigate freely
- No forced installs, copies, or downloads without express prior consent

### Privacy Requirements
- If collecting personal data: prominent privacy policy link required on landing page
- Privacy policy must state terms and conditions for data use
- Must comply with all applicable standard industry and legal requirements

### Terms & Conditions
- Advertisers facilitating sales must present clear terms and conditions

## 5. Trademark Policies

### General Rules
- Trademark and intellectual property infringement prohibited
- Third-party advertisers prohibited from using brands' trademarks in ways that infringe intellectual property rights
- Includes dynamic text insertion — cannot circumvent trademark rules

### Fair Use Exceptions
- Resellers of authentic goods or services may use trademarks in ad text (fair use)
- Must be a legitimate reseller of the trademarked products

### Image Trademarks
- Ads and websites must not contain images or logos that infringe copyright laws
- Trademarked images in ads trigger review and potential disapproval

## 7. Financial Services Rules

### General Requirements
- Must comply with all applicable local laws and regulatory requirements
- Transparency about risks and fees mandatory
- Claims require certification or verification
- Products making unrealistic claims are disallowed

### Specific Categories
- **Lending/Loans**: local regulatory compliance required; terms must be transparent
- **Investment products**: risk disclosures required; no unrealistic return promises
- **Insurance**: must comply with local insurance advertising regulations
- **Cryptocurrency**: heavily restricted; varies by market
- **Payday loans**: restricted with specific regional rules
- **Binary options**: heavily restricted
- **Penny stocks**: prohibited on Audience Network

### Compliance Enforcement
- Financial services violations trigger specific editorial codes (8186, 8192, 8198, 8204, 8210)
- Missing certification or accreditation causes immediate disapproval

## 8. Common Disapproval Reasons (From Editorial Reason Codes)

### Most Frequent Disapprovals
1. **Content policy violation** (codes 5, 32, 59, 64, 65, 90) — generic catch-all for disallowed content
2. **Excessive capitalization** (code 16, 45) — random or ALL-CAPS in ad copy
3. **Trademark infringement** (code 17, 8039) — unauthorized use of third-party IP
4. **Repeated punctuation/symbols** (code 19) — excessive !!!, ???, etc.
5. **Landing page not relevant** (code 24, 85, 349) — content doesn't match ad text
6. **Spelling errors** (code 54) — typos in ad title or text
7. **Grammar errors** (code 46) — grammatically incorrect ad copy
8. **Punctuation errors** (code 50) — incorrect or excessive punctuation
9. **Unsupported claims** (codes 92, 93, 362, 363) — claims without substantiation
10. **Pop-ups/pop-unders** (codes 56, 89, 117) — on landing page
11. **Website inaccessible** (codes 4, 39) — landing page not loading
12. **Insufficient content** (code 88) — thin landing pages
13. **Phone number formatting** (codes 7, 35, 38, 52, 62) — phone number issues in ad text
14. **Missing privacy policy** (codes 67, 119, 152, 153, 154) — collecting data without disclosure
15. **Style guideline violation** (code 48) — non-compliant ad style

### Image-Specific Disapprovals
- Image quality violations (code 101)
- Nudity or offensive content in images (codes 8234, 8246)
- Weapons in images (code 8240)
- Substance abuse/alcohol/drugs/tobacco in images (code 8252)
- Trademarked images (code 8258)
- Gambling content in images (code 8264)
- Pharmaceutical content in images (code 8270)

## 9. Recent Policy Changes (2025-2026)

### August 2025
- **Clinical trials ban**: Global prohibition on advertising clinical trials or experimental treatments across all ad types
- **Advertising Identification Verification (AIV)**: Expansion to all supported markets requiring advertiser verification

### October 2025
- **EU political advertising**: Regulation (EU) 2024/900 compliance required; political advertising declaration mandatory for all Microsoft Monetize customers
- **Ad Library**: Launch displaying Bing Ads within the EU (advertiser names, payment details, searchable by company or keywords)

### July 2023 (Major Update — Still Current)
- Vitamins and supplements allowed on Audience Network (with restrictions)
- Gambling and betting ads approved for Audience Network (with licensing)
- Belgium: complete gambling ad ban
- Ireland: watershed restrictions on gambling ads (5:30 AM-9:00 PM)
- Netherlands: gambling ads restricted to users 24+
- Three-strikes violation policy enforcement formalized
- Immediate suspension criteria for egregious violations clarified
- Hungary and Poland: adult advertising no longer allowed

### Enforcement Framework
- Ad and ad component disapprovals
- Store or product disapprovals
- Three-strikes policy for repeated violations
- Immediate suspension for egregious violations
- Single egregious violation can lead to immediate account termination

## 10. Ad Format Specs

### Expanded Text Ads (Search)
- Headline 1: 30 characters max
- Headline 2: 30 characters max
- Headline 3 (optional): 30 characters max
- Description 1: 90 characters max
- Description 2 (optional): 90 characters max
- Display URL: 2 path fields, 15 characters each
- Final URL required (destination URLs deprecated)

### Responsive Search Ads (RSA)
- Headlines: 3-15 headlines, 30 characters each
- Descriptions: 2-4 descriptions, 90 characters each
- All headlines and descriptions should be independently meaningful (rotation applies)

### Audience Ads (Native/Display)
- Headline: 30 characters
- Long headline: 90 characters
- Description: 90 characters
- Images required in multiple aspect ratios
- Subject to stricter content policies than Search ads

### Shopping Ads
- Product title, description, and images from product feed
- Prices must match landing page
- Product must be available for purchase
- Clear return and shipping policies required
