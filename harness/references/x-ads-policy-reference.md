## 1. Prohibited Content

Content that is **never allowed** under any circumstances. Ads promoting these will be immediately disapproved and may trigger account suspension.

### 1.1 Illegal Products & Services

- Illegal drugs and drug paraphernalia (with specific exceptions for cannabis — see Restricted Content)
- Counterfeit goods and replica products
- Products facilitating illegal activity
- Unlicensed financial products

### 1.2 Weapons & Weapon Accessories

- Firearms, ammunition, and explosives
- Combat weapons and accessories
- Weapon modification parts

### 1.3 Tobacco & Recreational Drugs

- Cigarettes, cigars, pipe tobacco, chewing tobacco
- E-cigarettes and vaping products (in most jurisdictions)
- Recreational drug products and accessories

### 1.4 Deceptive & Fraudulent Content

- Misleading or dishonest advertising
- Ads that make false or deceptive claims
- Bait-and-switch landing pages
- Fake system notifications or misleading UI elements
- Fake "close" buttons
- Malware, spyware, or deceptive software downloads

### 1.5 Misleading Health Claims

- Ads promising miracle cures or making unverified medical assertions
- Unsubstantiated health or wellness claims

### 1.6 Adult Sexual Content

- Explicit sexual content in ads (despite some tolerance in organic content)
- Pornographic material

### 1.7 Surveillance & Spyware

- Monitoring tools without consent
- Surveillance software or hardware marketed for covert use

### 1.8 Derogatory & Hateful Content

Content that is disrespectful, discriminatory, or harmful toward individuals or groups based on:
- Race, ethnicity, color, national origin
- Sexual orientation, sex, gender identity
- Religious affiliation, age, disability
- Medical/genetic conditions, veteran status
- Refugee status, immigrant status

Specific examples include:
- Inflammatory or provocative content inciting anger or violence
- Content exploiting situations for unfair advantage
- Offensive, abusive, demeaning, intimidating, or bullying content
- Support for organizations promoting hate, criminal, or terrorist content
- Mocking references to events affecting protected groups

### 1.9 Harmful Weight Loss / Body Image Content

- Ads promoting weight loss that impacts mental or physical health
- Content that body-shames customers
- Content encouraging, glamorizing, or promoting unhealthy or unsafe eating behaviors or eating disorders

### 1.10 Sensitive Events & Controversial Issues

- Ads must not profit from or exploit sensitive events, topics, or controversial issues
- Includes natural/industrial disasters, violent attacks, or issues with significant social/political impact

### 1.11 Unauthorized References

- Claims of affiliation, endorsement, or integration without permission
- Unauthorized use of proprietary terminology, branding, logos
- False claims about platform access or special privileges

### 1.12 Content Prohibited for Minors

- Age-inappropriate material targeting or accessible to minors
- Products/services restricted to adults shown without proper age-gating

## 2. Restricted Content

These categories **require pre-authorization** from X before campaigns can launch. Advertisers must obtain approval and comply with category-specific requirements.

### 2.1 Adult Content

- Sexual products and services allowed only with strict age-gating
- Limited to specific jurisdictions where permitted by law
- Sexual merchandise ads permitted in Brazil, France, Germany, Mexico, UK (as of July 2025)

### 2.2 Alcohol

- Subject to regional restrictions and age-gating requirements
- All alcohol content advertising prohibited in Thailand
- Alcohol advertising permitted in Taiwan (as of December 2025)
- Must comply with local legal drinking age requirements
- Must include responsible drinking messaging where required

### 2.3 Cannabis & CBD

**United States only (with restrictions):**
- Advertisers must be licensed by appropriate authorities and pre-authorized by X
- Only topical hemp-derived CBD products containing 0.3% or less THC may be sold through ads
- Targeting restricted to jurisdictions where advertiser is licensed
- Must not target customers under age 21
- **Prohibited in cannabis ads:**
  - Claims of efficacy or health benefits
  - Showing or depicting a cannabis product in use
  - Depicting people using or under the influence of cannabis
  - False or misleading claims

### 2.4 Drugs & Drug Paraphernalia

- Controlled substances and accessories restricted
- OTC drug advertising permitted in Ecuador (as of October 2024)
- Must comply with local pharmaceutical regulations

### 2.5 Financial Products & Services

- Banking, investment, and crypto offerings require pre-authorization
- Cryptocurrency exchanges, wallets, kiosks/ATMs, and debit/credit cards permitted in UK, France, Germany (with restrictions)
- Debt consolidation services allowed in Japan (as of October 2025)
- Financial products/services advertising permitted in Taiwan (as of July 2024)
- Blockchain game advertising permitted in Canada, Turkey (June 2024), and Thailand (February 2024)
- Must include appropriate risk disclaimers

### 2.6 Gambling & Games

- Wagering and gaming activities require pre-authorization and licensing
- Brick-and-mortar casino ads permitted in US, Philippines (with restrictions)
- Gambling ads permitted in: Ireland, Portugal, Romania (October 2025); Denmark, Greece, Serbia, Switzerland (June 2025)
- Gambling affiliate/aggregator ads permitted in: Brazil, Chile, Ireland, Peru (October 2025); US (with restrictions)
- Lottery advertising permitted in UAE (October 2025)
- Must comply with local licensing requirements
- Age-gating required

### 2.7 Healthcare & Pharmaceuticals

- Medical products and medications require pre-authorization
- Trend Takeover and Trend Takeover+ now permit medical products/services in the US
- Telemedicine services permitted in US, Saudi Arabia (January 2025), Kuwait, Oman, Qatar (October 2024)
- Cosmetic procedures advertising approved in South Korea (October 2025)
- Cosmetic service aggregator apps approved in Japan (March 2024)
- Must comply with local healthcare advertising regulations

### 2.8 Political Content

- Requires strict certification and compliance (see Section 3)
- Enhanced identity verification required
- Geographic restrictions apply

## 3. Political Ad Rules

### 3.1 Background

X reversed its 2019 political ad ban and now permits political advertising with strict requirements. Political advertising operates under a "free speech with guardrails" framework.

### 3.2 Definitions

**Political Content Ads:** Reference a candidate, political party, elected or appointed government official, election, referendum, ballot measure, legislation, regulation, directive, or judicial outcome.

**Political Campaigning Ads:** Ads that advocate for/against or directly appeal for votes or financial support for candidates, parties, elections, legislation, referendums, or ballot measures. Includes ads from registered PACs and SuperPACs.

### 3.3 Geographic Availability

**Political Content Ads permitted in:**
Argentina, Australia, Chile, Colombia, Ecuador, Ghana, India, Indonesia, Israel, Japan, Mexico, Peru, Saudi Arabia, South Africa, UAE, United Kingdom, United States

**Political Campaigning Ads permitted in:**
Argentina, Australia, Colombia, Ecuador, Ghana, Japan, Indonesia, Mexico, Panama, Peru, South Africa, United Kingdom, United States

**Political ads targeting Australia:** Permitted as of March 2025

### 3.4 Certification Requirements

- Pre-approval required before running political campaigning ads
- Government-issued ID verification required
- Application processing time: approximately 1-3 business days
- Indian government entities must obtain separate certification
- X may request additional verification documentation at any time

### 3.5 Targeting Restrictions

Political campaigning ads can only be bought by citizens or entities based in the target country. Permitted targeting options limited to:
- Location
- Age
- Gender
- Interests/keywords
- Custom audiences
- Follower look-alikes

### 3.6 Disclosure Requirements

- "Paid for by" funding attribution mandatory
- Must disclose whether authorized by a candidate or organization
- Must include sponsoring organization contact information (US)
- Geo-restricted to advertiser's registered jurisdiction
- Archived in public Ads Transparency Center for seven years

### 3.7 Prohibited Political Content

- False or misleading information about how to participate in an election
- Information intended to intimidate or dissuade people from participating in an election
- Information intended to undermine public confidence in an election
- Content that violates electoral silence periods

### 3.8 Allowed Ad Formats for Political Ads

Promoted Ads, X Amplify, X Takeover, X Live, DPA, and X Ad features only.

### 3.9 Exemptions

News publishers meeting exemption criteria may reference political content without advocacy components.

## 4. Targeting Restrictions

### 4.1 Account Requirements

- Posts must be public
- Deactivated or suspended accounts cannot advertise
- Must have a functional, live URL in bio that accurately represents the brand
- Profile and header images cannot be GIFs
- Advertisements targeting specific countries must use that country's local language

### 4.2 Anti-Discriminatory Targeting

- Housing, lending, and employment opportunity restrictions apply
- Cannot target or exclude based on protected characteristics for these categories

### 4.3 Sensitive Category Limitations

- Sensitive category targeting has specific limitations
- Must not use targeting to circumvent age-gating or geographic restrictions on restricted content

### 4.4 Prohibited Geographic Targets

Ads cannot target or originate from sanctioned regions:
- Cuba, Iran, North Korea
- Crimea Region of Ukraine
- Donetsk and Luhansk Regions of Ukraine

### 4.5 Available Regions

Africa, Asia Pacific, Europe, Latin America, Middle East, and North America (specific countries within each region).

### 4.6 Sensitive Events

During sensitive events, X may temporarily pause advertisements to/from particular locations.

## 5. Ad Format Requirements

### 5.1 Text Ads (Promoted Posts)

| Attribute | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Post copy | 280 characters max (23-char reduction per link) |
| Format | Native format matching standard X posts |
| Features | Supports likes, replies, favorites, reposts |

### 5.2 Image Ads

| Attribute | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Post copy | 280 characters (257 with link) |
| File types | PNG, JPEG (no BMP/TIFF; GIFs render static) |
| File size | 5 MB max |
| Aspect ratios | 1:1, 1.91:1, 4:5, 2:3, 16:9, 9:16 |
| With website card | 800x418 px (1.91:1) or 800x800 px (1:1) recommended |
| Website card headline | 70 characters max (50 recommended) |
| Standalone | 1200x1200 px (1:1) or 1200x628 px (1.91:1) recommended |

**Image Ads with App Buttons:**
- App title: 200 characters max
- CTAs: Install, Open, Play, Shop, Book, Connect, Order

**Image Ads with Conversation Buttons:**
- Hashtag: 21 characters max
- Pre-populated user post: 256 characters
- Headline: 23 characters max
- Thank you text/URL: 23 characters each

**Image Ads with Polls:**
- Poll options: 2-4 maximum
- Per option text: 25 characters max
- Duration: 5 minutes to 7 days

### 5.3 Video Ads

| Attribute | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Post copy | 280 characters |
| File types | MP4, MOV |
| File size | 1 GB max (under 30 MB recommended) |
| Duration | 15 seconds recommended; up to 2:20 supported |
| Video bitrate | 6,000-10,000k (1080p); 5,000-8,000k (720p) |
| Frame rate | 29.97 or 30 FPS (60 FPS acceptable) |
| Video codec | H.264, Baseline/Main/High Profile, 4:2:0 color |
| Audio codec | AAC LC |
| Thumbnail | PNG/JPEG, 5 MB max, matching video aspect ratio |
| Looping | Auto-loops if under 60 seconds |

**Vertical Video Ads:**
- Resolution: 720x1280 minimum; 1080x1920 maximum
- Aspect ratio: 9:16 primary (also 4:5, 2:3, 1:1, 1.91:1, 16:9)
- Frame rate: 60 FPS max
- Bitrate: 5-10 Mbps target (25 Mbps max)
- Appears in Immersive Media Viewer

### 5.4 Carousel Ads

| Attribute | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Slides | 2-6 (images or videos) |
| Post copy | 280 characters |
| Aspect ratios | 1:1, 1.91:1, 4:5, 2:3, 16:9, 9:16 |
| Image carousel | 800x418 px (1.91:1) or 800x800 px (1:1) recommended |
| Video carousel | 800x450 px (16:9) or 800x800 px (1:1) recommended |
| Links | One destination (single) or up to six (multi-destination) |
| Website title | 70 characters (50 recommended) |

- Must maintain consistent aspect ratio within carousel
- Mixed media (image + video) requires matching aspect ratios

### 5.5 X Amplify

**Pre-roll:**

| Attribute | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| File size | 1 GB max |
| Duration | 15 seconds recommended (up to 2:20) |
| Format | MP4/MOV |
| Aspect ratio | 1:1 recommended; 9:16 also recommended |
| Recommended size | 1200x1200 px (min 600x600 for 1:1) |

**Sponsorships:**

| Attribute | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| File size | 1 GB max |
| Duration | 6 seconds recommended (up to 2:20) |
| Format | MP4/MOV |
| Aspect ratio | 16:9 or 1:1 recommended |
| Recommended size | 1200x1200 px (min 600x600 for 1:1; min 640x360 for other) |

### 5.6 X Live

| Attribute | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Video codec | H.264/AVC |
| Bitrate | 9 Mbps recommended; 12 Mbps max |
| Audio codec | AAC-LC |
| Audio bitrate | 128 bps max |
| Resolution | 1280x720 recommended; 1920x1080 max |
| Frame rate | 30 FPS recommended; 60 FPS max |
| Keyframe interval | 3 seconds (OBS) |
| Description | 280 characters max (supports external links) |
| Carousel | 5 videos max (live, VOD, GIFs, photos) |
| Timeline post | 5 keywords/phrases max |

Supported encoders: Restream, Golightstream, Socialive, OBS, Wirecast, Teradek, Elemental, Vmix, StreamYard, Streamlabs

### 5.7 X Takeovers

**Timeline Takeover:**
- First ad of day at conversation top
- Supports: Image Ads, Video Ads, Carousel Ads, X Live, Promoted Ads with conversation buttons/polls, Branded Notifications

**Spotlight Takeovers:**

| Attribute | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Trend name | 20 characters max |
| Trend description | 30 characters max |
| Format | MP4 video or static image |
| Aspect ratio | 16:9 (1280x720 recommended) |
| File size | 5 MB image; 15 MB GIF |
| Companion ads | 3-6 required |

### 5.8 Branded Notifications

Three campaign formats:
- **Scheduled Notification:** CTA post + single scheduled notification (30-day max)
- **Subscription Notifications:** CTA post + multiple scheduled posts (30-day max per post)
- **Instant Notification:** CTA post + instant notification post (30-day max)

Requirements:
- All posts start with @HandleToReplyTo placeholder
- Must include opt-out instructions
- CTA post must describe content timing clearly
- No @mentions without explicit permission
- Available in US, Canada (all managed advertisers); global with X Next

### 5.9 Dynamic Product Ads (DPA)

Prerequisites:
- X Pixel or Conversion API implementation
- Track: Page View, Content View, Add to Cart, Purchase events
- Product catalog via Shopping Manager: up to 1M products or 8 GB
- Scheduled feeds for daily sync

### 5.10 General Creative Rules

- No additional @mentions in ad copy (minimizes distractions)
- Hashtags are now banned from X ads
- Multiple emojis are banned from X ads
- No excessive capitalization or broken grammar
- No sensationalized language
- All creative must match landing page content and intent

### 5.11 Character Limits Summary

| Element | Limit |
|---------|-------|
| Post copy | 280 chars |
| Link reduction | 23 chars |
| Hashtag (conversation button) | 21 chars |
| App title | 200 chars |
| Headline (Image/Video cards) | 70 chars (50 recommended) |
| Headline (Notifications) | 23 chars |
| Poll option | 25 chars |
| Conversation headline | 23 chars |
| Event description | 280 chars |
| Trend name | 20 chars |
| Trend description | 30 chars |

## 6. Verification & Checkmark Requirements

### 6.1 Mandatory Verification

One of the following is required to run ads:
- **Organizations/Government:** Verified Organizations program
- **Individuals:** X Premium subscription

### 6.2 Verification Tiers (Functional Impact)

| Checkmark | For | Ad Access |
|-----------|-----|-----------|
| **Blue** (X Verified / X Premium) | Individuals, small businesses | Basic ad formats only |
| **Gold** (Organizations) | Businesses, larger advertisers | High-spend campaigns, advanced formats (video carousels, branded hashtags) |
| **Grey** (Government) | Governmental and political entities | Government and political ad formats |

### 6.3 Verification Impact on Campaigns

- Accounts that lose verification status will have active ad campaigns **paused automatically** until verification is restored
- Accounts without clear business identity or that fail verification are blocked from starting ad campaigns
- Premium account ads receive higher engagement due to verification trust signals
- Reply prioritization benefits verified brands in conversational strategies

### 6.4 X Premium Effects on Advertising

- Premium subscribers see approximately 50% fewer ads in their feeds
- This affects reach/frequency models for professional audiences
- Verified brands receive trust signal benefits

## 7. Brand Safety Architecture

### 7.1 Three-Tier Brand Safety Slider

| Tier | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Conservative** | Maximum restrictions, verified accounts only, limited reach |
| **Standard** | Balanced approach (default setting for most advertisers) |
| **Expanded** | Maximum reach, includes controversial content |

### 7.2 Additional Safety Tools

- Keyword exclusion lists
- Handle-based blocklists
- Third-party verification: Integral Ad Science (IAS) and DoubleVerify partnerships

## 8. Common Rejection Reasons

### 8.1 Inauthentic Identity / Verification

- Accounts lacking clear business identity
- Failed X Premium verification process
- Missing required verification tier for ad format

### 8.2 Low-Quality / Deceptive Creative

- Broken links
- Misleading handles
- Low-resolution visuals
- Sensationalized or clickbait language

### 8.3 Policy Violation — Prohibited Content

- Material in any banned category
- Tobacco, weapons, illegal products
- Explicit sexual content

### 8.4 Editorial Quality Issues

- Excessive capitalization (ALL CAPS)
- Broken grammar
- Sensationalized language
- Multiple hashtags or emojis (now banned in ads)

### 8.5 Landing Page Mismatch

- Non-functional destination URLs
- Content that does not match ad messaging
- Deceptive or misleading landing pages
- Auto-downloads or malicious content

### 8.6 Insufficient Verification

- Account lacks required verification tier for the ad format
- Missing political ad certification
- Missing category-specific pre-authorization

### 8.7 Political Ad Failures

- Failure to properly categorize advocacy ads
- Missing "Paid for by" disclosure
- Targeting outside permitted jurisdictions
- Missing electoral silence compliance

## 9. Recent Policy Changes (2025-2026)

### 9.1 2026 Changes

**March 2026 — Paid Promotion Disclosure Rules:**
X significantly updated "Declare paid promotion" rules. Accounts failing to disclose commercial promotions face severe penalties.

### 9.2 2025 Changes

**December 2025:**
- Alcohol advertising now permitted in Taiwan with restrictions

**October 2025:**
- Debt consolidation services allowed in Japan
- Gambling ads permitted in Ireland, Portugal, Romania
- Gambling affiliate/aggregator ads permitted in Brazil, Chile, Ireland, Peru
- Lottery advertising permitted in UAE

**July 2025:**

**June 2025:**
- Gambling ads permitted in Denmark, Greece, Serbia, Switzerland

**March 2025:**
- Political ads targeting Australia now allowed

**January 2025:**
- Telemedicine services advertising permitted in Saudi Arabia

### 9.3 2024 Changes (Selected)

- Political ads allowed in Ghana, Panama, South Africa, Saudi Arabia, UAE
- OTC drug advertising in Ecuador
- Telemedicine in Kuwait, Oman, Qatar
- Crypto products/services in South Africa
- Financial products in Taiwan
- Blockchain game advertising in Canada, Turkey, Thailand

### 9.4 Structural Policy Shifts

- Political advertising re-enabled (reversed 2019 ban under prior Twitter management)
- Verification tiers now function as ad eligibility gates (not just prestige badges)
- Brand Safety slider expanded with third-party verification partnerships (IAS, DoubleVerify)
- Hashtags and multiple emojis banned in ad creative (distraction reduction policy)
- Stricter paid promotion disclosure enforcement
- X operates under "free speech with guardrails" — emphasizes post-hoc enforcement and contextual safety signals over preemptive removal

### 9.5 Automated Review Pipeline

All ads undergo automated review within 15-60 minutes, evaluating:
- Text content via NLP for prohibited terms
- Visual assets checked for explicit content and deceptive design
- Landing page functionality and malware verification
- Account history (prior violations trigger stricter scrutiny)
