> Last updated: March 2026
> Sources: Snap Advertising Policies, Snap Commercial Content Policy, Snap Business Help Center, industry compliance guides

This document covers every major policy area enforced by Snapchat Ads. Use it as a pre-flight checklist before creating any Snapchat ad content. Violations can result in ad rejection, campaign suspension, or account termination.

## 1. Prohibited Content

Content that is **never allowed** under any circumstances. Ads promoting these will be immediately rejected and may trigger account termination.

### 1.1 Illegal Products & Activities

- Illegal drugs and drug paraphernalia
- Products or services that facilitate or encourage illegal activity
- Fake IDs, counterfeit documents
- Essay writing services and academic dishonesty tools

### 1.2 Weapons

- Firearms, ammunition, and explosives
- Combat knives, switchblades, and concealed weapons
- Weapon modification accessories

### 1.3 Tobacco & Nicotine

- Cigarettes, cigars, pipe tobacco, chewing tobacco
- E-cigarettes, vapes, and vaping liquids
- Hookah products and accessories

### 1.4 Adult & Sexual Content

- Depictions or graphic descriptions of genitalia in any context
- Exposed nipples or bare buttocks
- Partially-obscured nudity
- Depictions of specific sex acts
- Dating services emphasizing casual sexual encounters
- Non-consensual sexual material
- Pornography in any form

### 1.5 Counterfeit Goods

- Fake items imitating brand trademarks, logos, or names
- Replica or knockoff branded goods

### 1.6 Misleading Financial Schemes

- Ponzi schemes, pyramid schemes
- Get-rich-quick schemes without legitimate basis
- Predatory lending promotions

### 1.7 Hateful & Discriminatory Content

- Hate speech that demeans, defames, or promotes discrimination or violence based on:
  - Race, color, caste, ethnicity, national origin
  - Religion, sexual orientation, gender identity
  - Disability, veteran status, immigration status
  - Socio-economic status, age, weight, or pregnancy status
- Extremist content undermining safety and inclusion

### 1.8 Misleading & Deceptive Content

- Deceiving calls to action
- Landing pages unrelated to advertised content
- Cloaking techniques (showing different content to reviewers vs. users)
- False or misleading claims about products/services
- Copyright or trademark violations

### 1.9 Unapproved Health Supplements

- Health supplements without proper certifications
- Products making unverified medical claims

## 2. Restricted Content

These categories **may be allowed** but require additional compliance, certifications, age-gating, or geographic restrictions. Advertisers must receive pre-authorization in most cases.

### 2.1 Alcohol

- Must not be directed at anyone under the legal drinking age in the target jurisdiction
- Must not be shown in jurisdictions where alcohol advertising is prohibited
- Must include responsible drinking messaging (e.g., "Please drink responsibly")
- Must not encourage excessive consumption
- Must not portray drinking as a solution to personal problems
- Must not show minors or appeal primarily to minors
- Geographic restrictions vary by country

### 2.2 Gambling & Gaming

- Applies to: online casinos, brick-and-mortar casinos, lotteries, daily fantasy sports, sweepstakes, gambling affiliates
- Also applies to any product or service (including mobile games) that asks users to pay to play games of chance for prizes with real-world value
- Advertisers must comply with all applicable licensing and registration requirements
- Must provide Snap with proof of current license or registration from regulatory authorities in each target jurisdiction
- Age-gating required (18+ or applicable legal age)

### 2.3 Financial Services

- Complex financial products (including cryptocurrency wallets and trading platforms) must not target anyone under the applicable legal age
- Must not be shown in jurisdictions where such content is not permitted
- Pre-authorization from Snap required for cryptocurrency advertising
- Must include appropriate disclaimers and risk disclosures

### 2.4 Healthcare & Medical

- Requires relevant authority certification
- Must not make unsubstantiated medical claims
- Pharmaceutical advertising subject to local regulatory requirements
- Must include appropriate medical disclaimers

### 2.5 Dating Services

- Must be targeted to users age 18 and over
- Cannot include provocative or overtly sexual content
- Cannot reference prostitution or escort services
- Must receive pre-authorization from Snap before running ads

### 2.6 Weight Loss Products

- Demands clinical substantiation for any claims
- Exaggerated claims will be rejected (e.g., "Lose 10 pounds in a week" without clinical evidence)
- Must not promote unhealthy body image or eating disorders
- Before-and-after imagery subject to strict guidelines

### 2.7 Political & Issue Ads

- Requires pre-authorization and disclosure (see Section 6)
- "Paid for by" identification mandatory
- Subject to geographic and targeting restrictions
- Banned entirely in the EU from October 1, 2025 (per EU Regulation 2024/900)

### 2.8 CBD / THC Products

- Subject to strict regional regulation
- Must comply with all local laws regarding promotion
- Pre-authorization required

## 3. Creative Requirements by Format

All Snapchat ads use **9:16 vertical format**. Keep critical content 150px from top and bottom edges.

### 3.1 Single Image Ads

| Attribute | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Dimensions | 1080 x 1920 px (720 x 1280 minimum) |
| File format | JPG or PNG |
| File size | Max 5 MB |
| Color mode | RGB, 72 DPI |
| Brand name | Max 25 characters |
| Headline | Max 34 characters |
| Description | Max 150 characters |

### 3.2 Single Video Ads

| Attribute | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Dimensions | 1080 x 1920 px (720 x 1280 minimum) |
| File format | MP4 or MOV |
| Video codec | H.264 |
| Duration | 3-180 seconds (under 10 seconds recommended) |
| File size | Max 1 GB (32 MB or less preferred) |
| Bitrate | Minimum 2 Mbps at 30 fps |
| Audio | AAC, 128 kbps+, stereo |

### 3.3 Story Ads

| Attribute | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Discovery tile | 360 x 600 px (3:5 ratio), max 2 MB |
| Card content | 1080 x 1920 px per card |
| Card count | 3-20 cards total |
| Video card duration | 3-180 seconds per card |
| Attachment title | Max 45 characters |
| Attachments allowed | Web links, app installs, long-form video (10 min), AR Lenses |

### 3.4 Collection Ads

| Attribute | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Hero creative | 1080 x 1920 px |
| Hero video format | MP4/MOV, max 1 GB |
| Hero image format | JPG/PNG, max 5 MB |
| Product tiles | Exactly 4 tiles |
| Tile minimum | 160 x 160 px (500 x 500+ recommended) |
| Tile format | 1:1 square, JPG/PNG |
| Tile title limit | 30 characters visible |

**Catalog requirements:** Product ID, Title, Description, Image URL, Product URL, Price, Currency. Update frequency: at least daily. All URLs must be HTTPS.

### 3.5 Commercials (Non-Skippable)

| Attribute | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Duration | Fixed 6 seconds (non-skippable) |
| Dimensions | 1080 x 1920 px |
| File size | Max 32 MB |
| Format | MP4/MOV, H.264, 30 fps |
| Audio | Required (sound-on environment) |

### 3.6 Dynamic Ads

- Pull from product catalogs automatically
- Template dimensions: 1080 x 1920 px
- Display single product or up to 4 products in grid/carousel layout
- Feed formats: CSV, TSV, or Google Sheets with daily updates

### 3.7 Spotlight Ads

| Attribute | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Dimensions | 1080 x 1920 px |
| Duration | 5-60 seconds recommended |
| Format | MP4/MOV, max 1 GB |
| Audio | Strongly recommended |

### 3.8 Filters (Geofilters)

**Static Filters:**

| Attribute | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Dimensions | 945 x 2048 px |
| Canvas (full) | 1080 x 2340 px (1080 x 1920 safe viewing area) |
| File format | PNG with transparency |
| File size | Under 300 KB |
| Screen coverage | Maximum 25% of screen |

**Animated Filters:**

| Attribute | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Dimensions | 720 x 1560 px |
| File format | GIF, looping seamlessly |
| Duration | 1-3 seconds (ideally under 20 frames) |

**Filter Design Rules:**
- Center of screen must remain clear for selfies
- Logo placement: top or bottom quarters only
- No URLs, QR Codes, Snapcodes, or Snapchat usernames

**Geofilter Location Requirements:**
- Minimum geofence: 20,000 sq ft
- Maximum geofence: 50,000,000 sq ft
- Duration: 1 hour minimum to 30 days maximum

### 3.9 AR Lenses

**Technical Requirements:**

| Attribute | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Tool | Lens Studio or Lens Web Builder |
| Export format | .lns file |
| Max file size | 8 MB compressed |
| Max texture resolution | 2048 x 2048 per texture |
| Polygon count | Under 10,000 recommended |
| Audio | MP3/WAV, under 1 MB per clip |
| Animations | Under 10 seconds |
| 2D assets | PNG with transparency, power-of-2 dimensions preferred |
| 3D models | FBX, OBJ, or glTF format, optimized for mobile |

**AR Lens Mandatory Rules:**
- A visible brand logo or brand name is required on all Face and World Lenses
- Recommended logo placement: top left or top right, under UI elements
- Avoid placement where logo may be obscured by UI elements or the carousel
- Snapchat adds a "SPONSORED" slug (visible for 2 seconds only, does not appear in user Snaps)

**AR Lens Prohibited Elements:**
- URLs
- QR Codes
- Snapcodes
- Snapchat usernames
- Profanity (with limited exceptions)
- Editing the Snapchatter's perceivable race or skin tone (unless transforming into a non-human fictional character)

### 3.10 General Creative Standards

- Eye-catching, vibrant visuals required
- Clear, concise messaging
- Minimal text overlays
- Prominent call-to-action buttons
- No flashing images that could harm viewers
- Professional appearance with legitimate business identity
- Consistent visual branding
- No low-resolution or pixelated imagery

### 3.11 Text Limits Summary

| Element | Limit |
|---------|-------|
| Brand Name | 25 characters |
| Headline | 34 characters |
| Description | 150 characters |
| Story Attachment Title | 45 characters |
| Collection Tile Title | 30 characters |

## 4. Targeting Restrictions

### 4.1 Available Targeting Options

- Demographics: age, gender, location
- Interest-based targeting
- Lookalike audiences
- Retargeting (from Snap Pixel or app events)
- Custom audiences

### 4.2 Prohibited Targeting Practices

- Advertising restricted products (alcohol, gambling, dating, financial products) to minors
- Misusing age or demographic parameters
- Geographic targeting violations in restricted regions
- Targeting users in jurisdictions where the advertised product/service is illegal

### 4.3 Age-Gating Requirements by Category

| Category | Minimum Age Target |
|----------|-------------------|
| Alcohol | Legal drinking age in target jurisdiction |
| Gambling | 18+ or legal gambling age |
| Dating services | 18+ |
| Sexually suggestive content | 18+ (or age of majority in target location) |
| Financial products (crypto) | Applicable legal age |
| General ads | 13+ (Snapchat minimum age) |

### 4.4 Privacy Requirements

- Advertisers must provide opt-out mechanisms for users
- Data collection must comply with relevant legal and regulatory requirements
- Must comply with EU GDPR and applicable privacy laws

## 5. Young Audience Protections

Snapchat's user base skews young (90%+ users in the 13-34 age range), making youth protections critical.

### 5.1 General Rules

- Snapchat is a 13+ platform — ads addressed to or intended to appeal to children under 13 will be rejected
- Ads must be suitable for the selected audience in each geographic area
- Restricted categories (alcohol, gambling, dating, etc.) must use age-gating to exclude minors

### 5.2 EU/UK Specific Protections (2025+)

- Most targeting and optimization tools are no longer available for personalizing ads to Snapchatters under 18 in the EU and UK
- Personalization for under-18 users is limited to: language settings, age, and location
- No behavioral targeting, interest-based targeting, or retargeting for minor users in the EU/UK

### 5.3 Content Standards for Youth-Visible Ads

- No content glamorizing substance use
- No content promoting unhealthy body image
- No misleading claims targeting younger demographics
- No pressure tactics or urgency-based manipulation targeting youth
- No content encouraging dangerous behaviors or challenges

## 6. Political & Advocacy Advertising

### 6.1 Definitions

**Election-related ads:** Ads about or paid for by candidates or parties for public office, ballot measures or referendums, political action committees, and ads that urge people to vote or register to vote.

**Advocacy/issue ads:** Ads concerning issues or organizations that are the subject of public debate at local, national, or global levels. Examples include: abortion, immigration, the environment, education, discrimination, guns.

### 6.2 Requirements

- All political ads must include a "Paid for by" message identifying the paying person or entity
- In the US: electoral ads must state whether authorized by a candidate or organization and include sponsoring organization contact information
- Disclaimer requirements vary by jurisdiction
- Must comply with all applicable election laws, campaign finance laws, copyright law, defamation law, and FEC regulations

### 6.3 Prohibited Political Content

- Content that harasses, intimidates, or threatens individuals
- Misleading or deceptive content or impersonation
- Content violating publicity, privacy, copyright, or IP rights of third parties
- Graphic violence or calls for violence
- Attacks relating to a candidate's personal life

### 6.4 Foreign National Restrictions

- Political ads may not be paid for directly or indirectly by foreign nationals or entities
- Limited exception: EU Member State entities may fund ads targeting other EU Member States

### 6.5 Transparency

- Snap maintains a public political ad library
- Snap may publicly disclose ad content, targeting details, delivery, spend, and campaign information

### 6.6 EU Ban (Effective October 1, 2025)

- Snap will not permit political advertising in the EU as defined under EU Regulation 2024/900
- This covers ads by, for, or on behalf of political actors (parties, candidates, etc.)
- Covers ads designed to influence elections, referendums, or legislative/regulatory processes
- **Exempt:** Messages from official Member State or EU sources strictly limited to organizing elections/referendums or promoting participation

## 7. Landing Page Requirements

- Must load quickly on mobile devices
- Must align with and accurately represent ad messaging
- No misleading content, deceptive overlays, or hidden redirects
- No pop-ups, auto-downloads, or auto-play audio
- Must not contain prohibited content
- Must be functional and transparent
- URL must match the advertised product/service

## 8. Common Rejection Reasons

### 8.1 Policy Violations

- Content violating community guidelines
- Promoting prohibited products/services
- Making misleading or deceptive claims

### 8.2 Exaggerated Claims

- Unsubstantiated promises (e.g., "Lose 10 pounds in a week" without clinical evidence)
- Health claims without proper substantiation
- Financial return promises without disclaimers

### 8.3 Low-Quality Creatives

- Poor resolution or pixelated imagery
- Excessive text overlay
- Offensive or inappropriate imagery
- Non-vertical format or incorrect dimensions
- Blurry or illegible text

### 8.4 Non-Compliant Landing Pages

- Misleading content on destination URL
- Unwanted pop-ups or auto-downloads
- Landing page content does not match ad content
- Broken or non-functional landing pages

### 8.5 Targeting Violations

- Advertising age-restricted products to minors
- Misusing demographic targeting parameters
- Targeting prohibited jurisdictions for restricted content

### 8.6 Missing Required Elements

- Missing "Paid for by" disclosure on political ads
- Missing responsible drinking messaging on alcohol ads
- Missing gambling license documentation
- Missing brand logo on AR Lenses

## 9. Recent Policy Changes (2025-2026)

### EU Political Ad Ban (October 2025)

Effective October 1, 2025, Snap no longer permits political advertising in the EU as defined under EU Regulation 2024/900 on transparency and targeting of political advertising. Only official Member State or EU election organization messages are exempt.

### EU/UK Youth Ad Personalization Restrictions (2025)

Most targeting and optimization tools are no longer available for personalizing ads for Snapchatters under 18 in the EU and UK. Personalization is limited to language, age, and location.

### Enhanced Advertising Transparency (2025)

For Snapchatters in the EU aged 18+, Snap introduced expanded "Why am I seeing this ad" features providing more details about ad targeting and allowing users to limit ad personalization.

### Digital Services Act Compliance (2023-2025)

Snap implemented multiple changes to comply with the EU's Digital Services Act (DSA):
- Enhanced transparency in ad delivery
- Expanded user controls over personalized advertising
- Strengthened content moderation and reporting mechanisms

### Review Process

- Dual-layer review system combining automated scanning with human evaluation
- Most ads reviewed within 24 hours; complex campaigns may require up to 72 hours
- Recommended to submit ads 48-72 hours before planned launch

### Enforcement Actions

Violations can result in:
- Ad rejection
- Campaign suspension
- Account termination
- Appeals possible with supporting documentation; human reviewers typically respond within several business days
